# What Tektologiya Is For In Bildung

This is a bounded positioning note for `tektologiya-vol1`.

It is not a cross-project doctrine and not a new ontology.

## What it offers

`Tektologiya` is the clearest local source in the repo for an explicit
organizational vocabulary.

After the completed volume-1 pass, the project now has working English for a
small mechanism-set:

- `conjugation`
- `ingression`
- `deingression`
- `selection`
- `mobile equilibrium`
- `law of leasts`
- `analytic sum`

That gives `bildung-2.0` a way to speak more precisely about:

- how parts belong to wholes
- what mediates between otherwise separate complexes
- where a system's bottleneck lies
- how preservation differs from stasis
- when analogy has or has not risen to structural explanation

In that sense, `Tektologiya` is the subproject best able to theorize the charter's
claim that the subprojects are `organs of one system`.

## What it must not do

The same strength creates the main danger.

Bogdanov's vocabulary should not be imported into the other texts simply because
it is compact and portable. If it starts flattening Jünger, Gómez Dávila,
Cassirer, or Dick into organizational reductions, it is being used badly.

The rule has to remain:

- use tektological language when another text has already generated the relevant
  organizational pressure
- do not let Bogdanov function as a standing master-framework for the repo
- do not confuse analogy with structural explanation

`5.3` is the local warning: analogy is necessary but insufficient until
structural ground is shown.

## What is already earned

- local use inside `tektologiya-vol1` itself
- volume-level consolidation of the main-text and front-matter arc
- visual work that clarifies the mechanism grammar
- a future bounded encounter with Rosen, where `organization`, `closure`, and
  system-preservation are already explicit on both sides

## What is not yet earned

- a general Bogdanov-first rereading of the other subprojects
- a five-text thread on `organization / mediation / crisis`
- promotion of tektological vocabulary into repo-wide architecture

If a cross-project Bogdanov thread begins soon, the most honest first version is
probably `Bogdanov <-> Cassirer`, because both projects are already asking how
knowledge becomes portable across domains and both have active translation work
behind them.

## Practical next uses

- curate and refine the first architectural visual packet
- build a compact mechanism poster after the first packet stabilizes
- run one bounded Rosen encounter as a contact test for the vocabulary
- pressure-test `fused / beaded` internally before pretending it has broad
  cross-project authority

## Status

Local positioning note. Keep it bounded and revisable.
