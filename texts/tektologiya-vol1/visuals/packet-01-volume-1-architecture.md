# Packet 01: Volume-1 Architecture

This is the first curated Tektologiya visual packet.

It is deliberately selective. The journal currently contains many visual
candidates, but the first packet should show the architecture of volume `1`
rather than scatter into local examples.

## Selection rule

Included here are the diagrams that best show one of the following:

- the volume-level arc
- the core mechanism grammar
- the turn from formative to regulating process
- the diagnostic threshold logic
- the methodological boundary between analogy and explanation
- the practical meaning of the law of leasts

Held back for later packets:

- local physical examples
- front-matter illustrations
- application-specific plates
- the larger single-sheet composite mechanism poster, which should come after
  the simpler plates are stabilized

## Packet logic

The packet can be read in order:

1. whole-volume arc
2. basic formative split
3. mediating bridge
4. regulating turn
5. diagnostic threshold
6. methodological caution
7. practical fork

That sequence should let a reader see what volume `1` is trying to build before
getting lost in examples.

## Rendering cautions

These ASCII drafts are placeholders for the first actual plates, not final
layout instructions.

- do not render plates `1`, `3`, `4`, and `6` as identical vertical ladders;
  the packet needs varied spatial grammar
- preserve the branching and corrective logic where it exists, especially in
  `2` and `7`
- rework `4` so the rendered plate shows the section's counterintuitive
  stability logic, not only a downward sequence
