## Claim

Weyl keeps biological wholeness, heredity, inward understanding, and evolution
visible without treating them as exceptions to physics or as completed vitalist
explanations.

## Heredity And Life

The gene becomes the provisional last unit of life because of
self-duplication. Viruses sharpen the boundary between living and nonliving
matter by behaving inertly outside living cells while showing self-duplication
and mutation inside them.

Quantum physics matters biologically wherever small atomic groups exert
organism-scale control effects. Mutation is the central case: atomic-scale
events can become hereditary macroscopic differences.

Schrödinger's aperiodic crystal supplies Weyl's image of stable order from
small-scale order, not thermodynamic order from disorder.

## Inward Understanding

`Verstehen von innen her` is legitimate as a guide to life-problems. Weyl does
not dismiss direct self-awareness, interpretive understanding of others, or
animal life as meaningless projections. But inward understanding cannot replace
objective constructive theory.

## Evolution

Appendix F makes evolution secondary in the order of explanation. It is the
capstone reached after law, morphology, and inner constitution, not the first
principle from which those are derived.

Modern genetics corrects Darwin's continuous hereditary variation through
recombination, particulate inheritance, mutation, and genotypic systems.
Origin-of-life and teleological plan-pressure remain open but speculative.

## Use Limits

Do not use Weyl as current biology. Use him as a philosophical source for how
to hold physical mediation and biological residue together without premature
closure.
