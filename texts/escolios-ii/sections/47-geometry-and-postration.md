# Section 47: Geometry and Postration (§1021-1040, pp. 178-181)

These pages strip confidence out of modern abstraction and keep returning to the same counterweight: embodied value, kneeling truth, and inherited interpretation.

---

## §1021
> El hombre actual no vive en el espacio y en el tiempo, sino en la geometría y los cronómetros.

**Close:** Contemporary man does not live in space and time, but in geometry and chronometers.

**Free:** Modern man no longer lives in space and time, but in geometries and timekeeping.

**Exegesis:**
The point is not simply quantification. It is displacement: lived world and lived duration are replaced by measurable abstractions that can be administered.

`tags: modernity, time, abstraction`

**Free:** To respect history is not to applaud whatever happens, but to prefer embodied value to the purpose that miscarries.

## §1028
> La opacidad de un hecho social no se disipa mientras nos contentamos con subsumarlo bajo una ley empírica. Mientras no lo colocamos en un esquema estructural a priori.

**Close:** The opacity of a social fact does not dissipate while we content ourselves with subsuming it under an empirical law. While we do not place it in an a priori structural schema.

**Free:** A social fact remains opaque so long as we are content to subsume it under an empirical law instead of placing it within an a priori structural pattern.

**Exegesis:**
This is one of the local hinges of the volume. Empirical recurrence does not yet count as understanding; one must still ask what structure makes the recurrence legible.

`tags: structure, sociology, opacity`

## §1034
> El arte es el más peligroso fermento reaccionario en una sociedad democrática, industrial y progresista.

**Close:** Art is the most dangerous reactionary ferment in a democratic, industrial, and progressive society.

**Free:** In a democratic, industrial, progressive society, art is the most dangerous reactionary ferment.

**Exegesis:**
Art matters here not because it preaches a platform, but because it preserves standards and perceptions that the progressive order cannot fully digest.

`tags: art, reaction, progress`

## §1038
> Sólo en la postración de hinojos se expresa la verdad del hombre.

**Close:** Only in kneeling prostration is the truth of man expressed.

**Free:** The truth of man is expressed only on his knees.

**Exegesis:**
The anthropological claim is as stark as possible: truth about man appears not at the point of self-assertion but in enacted abasement before what exceeds him.

`tags: man, kneeling, truth`

## §1040
> Si no heredamos una tradición espiritual que la interprete, la experiencia de la vida nada enseña.

**Close:** If we do not inherit a spiritual tradition that interprets it, the experience of life teaches nothing.

**Free:** If we do not inherit a spiritual tradition capable of interpreting it, life teaches us nothing.

**Exegesis:**
Experience is denied self-sufficiency here. What life yields depends on an inherited form of interpretation, not on mere accumulation of happenings.

`tags: experience, tradition, interpretation`
