# Section 37: Reaction and Complicity (§821-840, pp. 145-148)

The next turn is more openly reactionary, but what matters is less slogan than discrimination: contradiction, hierarchy, beauty, love, and sociology are all being reassigned their proper rank.

**Free:** Concessions are the steps leading up to the scaffold.

## §824
> Las revoluciones de la izquierda sólo cambian el orden de los naipes. La revolución es inútil mientras no se inventen barajas nuevas con palos inéditos.

**Close:** The revolutions of the left only change the order of the cards. Revolution is useless so long as new decks with unprecedented suits are not invented.

**Free:** Left-wing revolutions only reshuffle the cards. Revolution remains useless until new decks are invented with suits no one has seen before.

`tags: revolution, left, deck`

## §825
> El reaccionario, hoy, es el antípoda del conservador. Es decir: del defensor de la democracia burguesa de ayer contra la democracia pequeño-burguesa de mañana. Pero el reaccionario nada espera de una revolución. Cuando el tedio y el asco engendren tiempos propicios, la reacción no será trivialmente revolucionaria sino radicalmente metanoiática.

**Close:** The reactionary today is the antipode of the conservative. That is: of the defender of yesterday's bourgeois democracy against tomorrow's petty-bourgeois democracy. But the reactionary expects nothing from a revolution. When boredom and disgust engender propitious times, reaction will not be trivially revolutionary but radically metanoic.

**Free:** Today the reactionary is the conservative's antipode: the conservative defends yesterday's bourgeois democracy against tomorrow's petty-bourgeois democracy. The reactionary expects nothing from revolution. When boredom and disgust generate favorable times, reaction will be not trivially revolutionary but radically metanoic.

**Exegesis:**
This is one of the few places where the label is defined directly. The crucial move is from political reversal to conversion: reaction is not a counter-revolutionary mirror image but a metanoic refusal of the whole field.

`tags: reactionary, conservative, metanoia`

**Close:** The intelligent leftist admits that his generation will not build the perfect society, but he trusts a future generation. His intelligence discovers his personal impotence, but his leftism prevents him from discovering the impotence of man.

**Free:** The intelligent leftist admits that his own generation will not build the perfect society, but he trusts a future one. His intelligence discovers his personal impotence, but his leftism prevents him from discovering man's impotence.

## §830
> Jerarquía es el principio que salva las contradicciones.

**Close:** Hierarchy is the principle that saves contradictions.

**Free:** Hierarchy is the principle that rescues contradictions.

**Exegesis:**
This is a crucial counterclaim to flattening thought. Contradictions are not abolished by hierarchy but preserved from mutual destruction within an ordered field.

`tags: hierarchy, contradiction, order`

## §832
> No hay que hablar de belleza de la religión, ni de religión de la belleza, sino de complicidad entre belleza y religión.

**Close:** One must not speak of the beauty of religion, nor of the religion of beauty, but of complicity between beauty and religion.

**Free:** We should speak neither of the beauty of religion nor of the religion of beauty, but of the complicity between beauty and religion.

**Exegesis:**
The aphorism blocks two easy reductions at once. Beauty is not simply an ornament of religion, and religion is not simply aestheticized beauty; they meet as accomplices.

`tags: beauty, religion, complicity`

## §833
> La pasión más ardiente no engaña, si conoce la inadecuación de su objeto. El amor no es ciego cuando ama locamente, sino cuando olvida que aún el irreemplazable ser amado sólo es una misteriosa primicia. El amor que no se cree justificado no es traición, sino propedeútica.

**Close:** The most ardent passion does not deceive if it knows the inadequacy of its object. Love is not blind when it loves madly, but when it forgets that even the irreplaceable beloved is only a mysterious first-fruit. Love that does not believe itself justified is not betrayal, but propedeutic.

**Free:** The most ardent passion does not deceive if it knows the inadequacy of its object. Love is blind not when it loves madly, but when it forgets that even the irreplaceable beloved is only a mysterious first fruit. Love that does not believe itself justified is not betrayal, but preparation.

**Exegesis:**
This is one of the densest love aphorisms in the volume. It preserves passion by denying it ultimacy: the beloved remains first fruit, not total possession or final justification.

`tags: love, passion, inadequacy`
