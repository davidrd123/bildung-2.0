## Why this unit

`Urgrund und Person` closes the translated run and already has enough local apparatus to support a real second pass:

- the part is closed at `§186`
- the main late glossary pressures (`Urgrund`, `Weltplan`, `der Hinzutretende`) already have evidence chains
- the journal has tracked the arc in four bounded batches rather than only as one late blur
- `thread:time-crisis` now reaches the end of the book through this part

The right second-pass question here is therefore not:

- is each English sentence acceptable?

It is:

- does the English preserve the unit's movement from withdrawal and institutional survival, through earth-power and maternal reversal, into spirit, cult, and hope, or does it leave the reader with a loose heap of church remarks and late metaphysical intensities?

## Pressure Clusters

### 1. `§§167-170` - Withdrawal, institution, second religiosity

Main risk:

- the opening can flatten into cultural conservatism or generic church-defense, instead of showing a precise situation: the personal gods withdraw, institutions outlast contents, and second religiosity can retard total politicization without restoring revelation

What has to stay visible:

- the father cannot simply be carried across
- `Dieu se retire` corrects a cruder formula of absence
- the church matters here first as institution and preserving structure, not as immediate salvation-treasury
- `Second Religiosity` and second `décadence` are not mere decline words; they mark a delayed, preservative, and retarding phase

Draft-level watchpoints:

- English that makes the church-language devotional rather than structural
- wording that turns `Second Religiosity` into mere religious revivalism
- phrasing that loses the architectural force of `Great Houses`, reservoirs, shelters, and delayed structures

### 2. `§§171-175` - Church and total situation, faith and emptiness

Main risk:

- the middle can split into separate mini-topics - church, law, faith, nihilism - instead of showing one connected field: institution versus total situation, paternal afterworking, belief as gift, and emptiness rather than nothing

What has to stay visible:

- church as institution and metaphysical total situation are not the same thing
- law rests on truths juridical procedure cannot establish
- `der Hinzutretende` returns here in a stronger register than astrology alone
- faith is not merit, proof, or will
- emptiness is not nothing, and the gap in `Weltplan` is internal rather than merely psychological

Draft-level watchpoints:

- smoothing `Gesamtlage` into vague background
- wording that turns `der Hinzutretende` into prophet, expert, or spiritual adviser
- English that lets `Leere` slide into generic nihilism
- language that turns `Unergänzte` into a neat abstract noun instead of keeping the productive awkwardness

### 3. `§§176-180` - Worker, materialism, mother-right, transit nihilism

Main risk:

- this run can be read as one more anti-materialist complaint when the actual movement is stranger: rationalism clears, materialism names deeper earth-power, the mother embodies `Urgrund`, and nihilism is only a transit force

What has to stay visible:

- `Arbeiter` is metaphysical `Gestalt`, not class sociology
- `Vorweisung` and apparatus-existence matter through advance display, not only through effects
- materialism has visible and invisible currents
- mother-earth and son are to be seen in mirrored simultaneity, not in cause and effect
- nihilism wills a determinate paternal order away rather than loving pure nothing

Draft-level watchpoints:

- English that sociologizes the worker
- phrasing that turns materialism into flat doctrine only
- wording that weakens the maternal register into mere symbolism
- smoothing `Durchgangsstation` into an existential cliche instead of a real transit image

### 4. `§§181-186` - Sacred time, earth-experiment, spirit, coda

Main risk:

- the closing can turn pious, abstract, or consolatory instead of remaining a sharp late recomposition of the whole unit

What has to stay visible:

- world religions outlast states because they remain more tightly bound to `Weltplan`
- the `Urgrund` begins to stir before the question is asked
- the earth's true partner is not `Verstand` but `Geist`
- the church's deeper footing is not reducible to apologetics
- cultic abbreviation and `der Hinzutretende` answer a mismatch between faith and consciousness
- the final preference for hope over fear is a closing orientation, not a sentimental softening

Draft-level watchpoints:

- English that makes `Geist` versus `Verstand` sound like generic spirituality versus reason
- smoothing `auf Vorrat gedacht` into flat foresight
- language that turns `Abbreviaturen` into mere shorthand rather than proven cultic aids
- a coda that sounds merely uplifting instead of hard-won

## Term Watchpoints

### `term:urgrund`

The reread should not try to settle the English once and for all. What needs checking is whether the local English keeps:

- earth-power rather than merely metaphysical depth
- the maternal and cosmogonic registers together
- the later methodological pressure in `§182`, where questioning responds to a prior stirring

### `term:weltplan`

The label `world-plan` does not need reopening by default. What needs checking is whether local passages keep:

- distinction from `Staatsplan`
- the difference between relational ordering and reified hidden agent
- the gap, transit, and sacred-time registers opened late in the part

### `term:hinzutretende`

The current rendering is still the best local choice because it keeps addition visible. The reread should guard against any local sentence that turns the figure into:

- expert
- prophet
- priestly office
- spiritual consultant

when the point is the added relation and interpretive completion itself.

## Image Watchpoints

- architectural language is structural, not decorative: houses, reservoirs, shelters, outer works, foundations
- paternal and maternal language is structural, not merely mythic coloring
- water and tidal language are structural, not decorative: ebb, attraction, reserves, power-station, flood
- earth and experiment belong together structurally in the late arc
