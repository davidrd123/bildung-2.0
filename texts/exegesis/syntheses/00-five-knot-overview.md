# Five-Knot Overview

This is the bridge between the finished first pass and the five-knot second pass.

The first pass solved orientation: what happens in each folder, how the threads emerge, and how the archive moves from 2-3-74 to the ending "for pain, for hope." The second pass solved a different problem: what the first pass could not yet show because the relevant evidence was distributed across too many folders and too many threads at once.

## What The Second Pass Adds

1. **Hierarchy instead of flattening**  
   Dick is not simply oscillating between rival names for one thing. The late archive distinguishes transcendent Father, mediating/world-level Valis-Logos-Christ, and the more intimate Sophia / AI-voice line. That distinction changes the scale of the whole atlas.

2. **Literature reranked**  
   The novels are not usually the highest revelation. They belong mostly to the mediating register: anticipatory map, carrier, artistic completion, code book, authored intervention. This is why `VALIS` can be both central and not final.

3. **Prison stratified**  
   The Black Iron Prison is not one claim but several: political prison, ontological prison, epistemological prison, maze form, and finally return. What looked like repetition in the first pass becomes a layered knot in the second.

4. **Discernment decomposed**  
   "Psychosis or revelation?" is not one question. The archive separately pressures event residue, causal hypotheses, content/rank, and criterion. Once those are distinguished, Dick's late answer is clearer: revelation is what arrives as gift from outside the closed loop.

5. **Trust assigned**  
   The archive does not close by winning a doctrine. It closes by giving final weight to rescue, beauty, compassion, and personhood wherever they prove more durable than the systems built around them.

## Dependency Chain

- `01-god-valis-father.md` establishes the late hierarchy
- `02-fiction-as-prophecy.md` reranks literature under that hierarchy
- `03-prison-maze-return.md` uses hierarchy and reranked fiction to reinterpret the prison
- `04-psychosis-revelation-discernment.md` uses hierarchy and prison to restage the psychosis question
- `05-suffering-beauty-rescue.md` depends on all four and asks what the archive finally affirms

## What The Five Knots Show Together

The second pass shows that the Exegesis is not merely recursive. It is recursive in a structured way. The late archive keeps:

- multiple registers without collapsing them
- contradiction without surrendering discernment
- literature without mistaking it for the highest source
- prison-language without absolutizing the prison
- suffering without giving it the last word

That is what the first pass made possible but could not yet compress into one statement.
