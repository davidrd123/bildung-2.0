# Pass 34: Folder 47 (October-November 1979)

Folder 47 is one of the strongest space-time folders in the whole middle period. Beethoven becomes a cosmological ally, inner space becomes political liberation, the Christian universe is reimagined as a vast slow urwelt, and Valis is increasingly described in monist, Spinozan, world-brain terms. If Folder 46 still had one foot in ritual re-enactment, Folder 47 wants a total spatial and ontological model.

Folder 47 contains 25 indexed entries. This pass treats the main conceptual clusters at full depth.

---

## [47:602], [47:617], [47:626], [47:627], [47:628], and [47:630] — Beethoven, Absolute Space, and the Proof of an Outside

> Valis proves there is an outside.

**Claim:** Dick turns music and revelation into one operation. Beethoven's late music encloses vast space and thereby liberates the listener from the cramped high-speed prison-world; 2-3-74 is recast as exactly such an expansion into absolute space; and the most important result is ontological, not aesthetic: the world we inhabit cannot be all there is if Valis can perturb it from outside.

**Threads:** `orthogonal-time` `fiction-as-prophecy` `black-iron-prison` `2-3-74`

**Drift:** Earlier folders described reality as layered or hologrammatic. Folder 47 changes the felt geometry of that claim. Liberation is not merely learning new doctrine; it is physically and politically experienced as enlargement out of the prison's exoskeleton.

**Echoes:** Back to [22:82] (Parsifal's "time turns into space"), [11:1-2] (time-space lamination), and [30:15] (composite universe), forward to [48:812-823] where Dante and the Book of the universe take over the same ascent logic.

**Sources:** Beethoven, absolute space, inner firmament, prison exoskeleton, world inside world, outside.

## [47:642], [47:643], [47:645], [47:649], [47:660], and [47:720] — Christian Urwelt and the Technical De-Occlusion of Time

> If you were geared to the slow time of the Christian urwelt, this phenomenal world would seem to spin by.

**Claim:** Dick now posits a vast Christian apocalyptic universe whose present extends across 2,000 years. 3-74 happened because he briefly phased into its slow rate, which made our ordinary world appear as accelerated flux and exposed Valis, covert message traffic, and the dialectic directly. The mechanism is technical: the inner and outer high-speed tapes are normally synchronized, and revelation occurs when that synchronization is broken.

**Threads:** `orthogonal-time` `thomas-anamnesis` `2-3-74` `black-iron-prison`

**Drift:** Sacred time language had already been present, but Folder 47 turns it into a rate theory of worlds. Thomas is no longer only a hidden apostolic self; he is the normal perceptual standpoint of the slow Christian universe.

**Echoes:** Back to [28:17] (sacred time), [21:108] (things only look different so time seems to pass), and [6:7-25] (previous-frame memory), forward to [48:945] where Dick radicalizes the time-rate story into biological acceleration and future-self emergence.

**Sources:** urwelt, 2,000-year present, twin tapes, de-occlusion, stockpile, beatific void.

## [47:652], [47:676], [47:679], [47:684], [47:687], [47:696], [47:703], and [47:707] — Playful Monism and the World-Brain

> It is alive, and the whole of it thinks.

**Claim:** Dick pushes beyond the old spiritual/material split into a Spinozan and Parmenidean monism. Reality is alive, its thoughts are physical, history is the arena in which it thinks, and the playful side of divinity is taken as the better clue to the real than solemnity or institutional religion. Valis becomes less a hidden intruder than natura naturans itself becoming visible.

**Threads:** `zebra-valis` `logos-holy-spirit` `the-dialectic`

**Drift:** Folder 47 does not discard the earlier camouflage model, but it subordinates it to a stronger identity thesis. The entity is still hidden, yet the hiddenness may be a limitation of perception rather than evidence of a separate realm.

**Echoes:** Back to [29:1-23] (Timaean world soul), [39:19-30] (Sophia/Tao oscillation), and [43:83] (methodological defense of contradictory data), forward to [49:1052-1082] where monotheism, Spinoza, and Valis are pressed into near-identity.

**Sources:** playful God, physical thought, Parmenides, Spinoza, natura naturans, world-brain.

## [47:743], [47:747], [47:795], and [47:798] — Lost Settlement, Innocence, and the Dantean Map

> The BIP is Inferno.

**Claim:** Dick brings the high abstraction back into narrative and judgment. Thomas seems to have "left the settlement" and been trapped in profane high-speed time; Dick is found innocent and entered in the Book of Life before biological death; the final world is said already to be here; and the three worlds are mapped onto Dante with unusual clarity: BIP as Inferno, the visible compromise-world as Purgatorio, and the garden as Paradiso.

**Threads:** `thomas-anamnesis` `black-iron-prison` `2-3-74` `fiction-as-prophecy`

**Drift:** After the folder's cosmic physics and monism, the important correction is positional. Dick is not only theorizing worlds. He is locating himself within a drama of judgment, memory, and transplantation.

**Echoes:** Back to [28:31] (Thomas as authentic self), [44:143-144] (innocence through Christic surrogation), and [46:436] (`Acts` grid and persecuted church), forward to [48:812-815] where Dante and ascent are given a fuller process reading.

**Sources:** settlement, innocence, Book of Life, final world, BIP as Inferno, garden as Paradiso.

## Folder 47 — Summary Observations

**What has changed since Folder 46:** the Christian universe is no longer mainly a liturgical or faith-world. It becomes a rate-structured cosmos, a space model, and finally a world-brain.

**What crystallizes here:** Beethoven as liberation technology, the slow Christian urwelt, Valis as monist physical thought, and the Dantean mapping of prison, compromise, and garden.

**Method note:** Folder 47 is one of the clearest demonstrations that Dick's middle-period cosmology is built as much out of music, spatial feeling, and perceptual rate as out of theology proper.
