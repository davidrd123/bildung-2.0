# Pass 31: Folder 44 (Spring 1979 - January 1980)

Folder 44 is the beginning of the long continuous numbered run that extends through Folder 49, and it immediately becomes one of the most searching late blocks. Dick revisits the initial 2-74/3-74 blitz, stages the strongest psychosis-only case against his own experience, partly abandons the Jane hypothesis through adoptionist correction, returns to living scripture and Empire as ossified complex, and finally offers one of his clearest surrogate-death accounts of what happened in 3-74.

Folder 44 contains 7 indexed entries. This pass treats the main conceptual clusters at full depth.

---

## [44:12] — The Blitz and Obligingly Plastic Reality

> Reality could be tricked into contradicting itself.

**Claim:** Dick returns to the primordial intuition behind 2-74 and 3-74: reality is not inert but obligingly adaptive, and under the right push-pull conditions it can be induced to expose its own contradictions. This does not mean arbitrary wish-fulfillment. It means that a sentient and mimetic field can be coaxed into disclosing softer points where another order shows through.

**Threads:** `2-3-74` `the-dialectic` `orthogonal-time`

**Drift:** Earlier folders had already made world plastic, pattern-responsive, and revision-prone. Folder 44 grounds those claims in the original blitz itself. The key novelty is methodological: Dick insists that what mattered was not refusal of reality testing but hyper-attentive dialogue with reality, feeling for where the mimicking field would yield.

**Echoes:** Back to [6:44–49] (plastic reality and deliberate modulation), [13:41–44] (binary reality as knowledge), [43:83] (reality testing inside contradiction), and [39:30] (oscillating models), forward to the Q&A cross-examination at [44:63].

**Sources:** blitz, obliging reality, push-pull dialogue, mimetic field, continuity contradiction.

---

## [44:63] and [44:68] — Psychosis Cross-Examination and Adoptionist Correction

> The enigma remains.

**Claim:** Dick conducts the most rigorous reductionist interrogation yet of 2-3-74 and finds that it still will not close. The psychosis account explains archaism, animism, and syntonic revelation only by contradiction; problem-solving remains unresolved; the female twin-sister hypothesis emerges as a tempting solution; and then the adoptionist voice partially corrects even that, pushing the source back toward God rather than merely familial residue.

**Threads:** `2-3-74` `logos-holy-spirit` `zebra-valis`

**Drift:** Folder 42 had already diminished the heroic self, and Folder 43 defended staying with contradiction. Folder 44 combines both moves in a more exacting way. The archive does not simply oscillate because Dick is indecisive. It oscillates because every total explanation generates a new residue it cannot absorb. Here the residue is problem-solving, adoption, and the persistent reality of the other mind.

**Echoes:** Back to [18:23] (psychosis hypothesis), [10:49–98] (female advocate and secret deity), [39:8] (scramble by healers), and [43:83] (enigma as both intelligence and acosmism), forward to [44:143–144] where the source becomes overtly Christic again.

**Sources:** Platt tape, psychosis, syntonicity, Jane, adoptionism, other mind.

---

## [44:73] and [44:82] — Living Scripture and Empire as Ossified Complex

> "Acts" is/are alive and can replicate.

**Claim:** Dick reconnects the experience to living textual ontology and to his oldest prison diagnosis. Scripture is treated as a preexistent living being whose literal body conceals occult soul; `Acts` and the plasmate belong to that order; and the Empire is redescribed as an ossified complex or psychotic frozen mind invaded by a dissolving metabolic toxin.

**Threads:** `logos-holy-spirit` `black-iron-prison` `fiction-as-prophecy` `the-dialectic`

**Drift:** The important return here is to `Tears` and `Acts` as diagnostic instruments rather than merely symbolic references. Folder 44 ties the living-text model directly to the old BIP claim: if nothing new has happened since Acts, then Empire is a stuck mind. Salvation becomes not abolition of reality but dissolution of the iron complex that has monopolized it.

**Echoes:** Back to [22:39–46] (living information in texts), [25:21] (fiction dramatizing the two powers), [13:45–46] (living thought-entities), and [6:41–49] (the world as quasi-postmortem thought-field), forward to Folder 45's cosmic Christ and world-melting.

**Sources:** esoteric Torah, Philo, `Acts`, King Felix, Empire as complex, `Flow My Tears, the Policeman Said`.

## [44:143] and [44:144] — Surrogate Death and Stereo Vision

> Christ took my place in 3-74.

**Claim:** Dick gives one of his clearest late accounts of the event as literal surrogation. He died briefly, returned, and lives because Christ accepted the death wound in his place; Thomas is identified with that Christic substitute; and the result is a doubled consciousness able to see both worlds at once.

**Threads:** `2-3-74` `thomas-anamnesis` `logos-holy-spirit`

**Drift:** Earlier folders had offered creator-in-amnesia, track-switch repair, implantation, and adoptionist correction. Folder 44 returns to a more recognizably Christian but still highly Dickian solution: not juridical atonement in the abstract, but literal substitution inside the death trail so the programmed victim survives and enters stereo vision. It is a powerful re-centering move after the psychosis and Jane detours.

**Echoes:** Back to [20:36–38] (Christological correction), [10:49–83] (Thomas and second birth), [13:21–22] (higher will and tragic rectification), and [29:31–46] (intervention rather than explanation), forward to Folder 45's cosmic Christ and death-strip surgery.

**Sources:** brief death, born again, Christ surrogation, Thomas, stereo vision, Kingdom.

---

## Folder 44 — Summary Observations

**What has changed since Folder 43:** Dick stops defending contradiction in the abstract and forces it through a sequence of candidate explanations: plastic reality, psychosis, Jane, adoption, living text, ossified Empire, and finally surrogate Christ.

**What crystallizes here:** the strongest late psychosis challenge to the archive, `Acts` as living scripture, Empire as frozen complex invaded by new life, and 3-74 as surrogative death plus returned life.

**Method note:** Folder 44 begins a long continuous entry that runs through later folders. The atlas should treat this pass as the opening movement of that extended sequence, not as a self-contained endpoint.
