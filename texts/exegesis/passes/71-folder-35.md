# Pass 71: Folder 35 (July 1977)

Folder 35 is brief and methodologically rich. It asks why revelation does not simply clear everything up, then answers by redefining what revelation does: it points to the thing itself rather than replacing it with explanation. Dick immediately joins that point to his own fiction, above all `Time Out of Joint`, where fake world, hidden time segment, and transtemporal self had already rehearsed the 3-74 pattern.

Folder 35 contains 2 indexed entries.

---

## [35:3] - The Oracle Gives a Sign, Not a Final Explanation

**Claim:** Dick finally asks a sharp question: if the entity can transfer deep knowledge directly, why does it not simply explain itself once and for all? His answer is one of the best methodological statements in the mid-period archive. The entity is not itself obscure. It is only obscure relative to our limited faculties. Revelation therefore does not abolish mystery; it indicates it. Like the oracle in Heraclitus, it gives a sign and points toward the thing itself.

**Threads:** `the-dialectic` `logos-holy-spirit`

**Drift:** This is a mid-period precursor to Folder 43's defense of contradictory data and to the late koan-account of `VALIS`.

**Echoes:** Forward to [43:83] (contradictory data retained), [64:E-20] (paradox as engine), and [84:8#2] (novel as koan rather than statement).

**Sources:** mystery, revelation, oracle, sign, ontological core, limited conceptual faculties.

## [35:6] - `Time Out of Joint`, Transtemporal Self, and the Bible as Door

**Claim:** Dick rereads `Time Out of Joint` as one of the clearest anticipations of 3-74. The fake world is really another time segment; the self spans beyond the present; and scripture functions not as pious supplement but as passage into the real world where Dick understands himself. This is one of the cleanest statements of how fiction, identity, and time all interlock in the corpus.

**Threads:** `fiction-as-prophecy` `orthogonal-time`

**Drift:** Folder 32 will soon generalize this into an explicit Gnostic account of Dick's fiction. Folder 35 gives the transitional recognition scene.

**Echoes:** Back to [4:220] (`Ubik` as prior contact), [31:18] (the corpus completed by 3-74), and forward to [32:4-7] (`Ubik`, `Maze`, and `Stigmata` as aggregate Gnostic disclosure).

**Sources:** `Time Out of Joint`, fake world, true temporal locus, transtemporal psyche, Bible as door.

## Folder 35 - Summary Observations

**What changes in July 1977:** Dick clarifies both what revelation can do and what his fiction had already been doing.

**What crystallizes here:** mystery as sign rather than solved object, and `Time Out of Joint` as one of the strongest anticipatory models of 3-74.

**Method note:** Folder 35 helps explain why the Exegesis never converges. Revelation points; it does not replace reality with a final verbal package.
