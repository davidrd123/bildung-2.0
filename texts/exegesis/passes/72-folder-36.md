# Pass 72: Folder 36 (August-September 1977)

Folder 36 contains one major entry and one marker-only remainder, but the major entry is unusually strong. It gives a three-layer ontology in which Christian writing or Logos lies beneath fleshly nature, which itself lies beneath the artificial technological world. Reality is thus textual, natural, and manufactured all at once, with each upper layer hiding the lower source that generates it.

Folder 36 contains 2 indexed entries. [36:29] survives only as a marker in the abridgement.

---

## [36:18] - Logos Below Flesh Below Technology

**Claim:** Dick dreams a three-level world: hidden Christian writing at the base, flesh and nature above it, and the artificial electronic world above that. The key claim is causal. The written Logos does not merely describe reality; it generates the natural layer, which then gives rise to the technological one. This makes Christianity not an interpretation of the world but its deep punched tape or source-code. Once more Dick's own fiction becomes indispensable, because `The Electric Ant`, `Maze`, and Persus 9 already contain the model.

**Threads:** `logos-holy-spirit` `fiction-as-prophecy` `black-iron-prison`

**Drift:** Folder 31 had already joined writing and 3-74 into one event. Folder 36 makes the writing-world relation ontological. Folder 64 will later restate the same logic as scripture and reality in object-mode relation.

**Echoes:** Back to [31:18] (writing as upward thrust), [32:4-7] (fiction dissolving the world), forward to [64:E-12-20] (world/book equivalence) and [91:1-19] (technical disclosure through `BTA`).

**Sources:** hidden Christian writing, flesh wall, electronic artifact, punched tape, `Electric Ant`, `Maze`, Persus 9, Logos substratum.

---

## Folder 36 - Summary Observations

**What changes in late summer 1977:** the relation between scripture, nature, and technological world becomes layered and causal rather than merely analogical.

**What crystallizes here:** Logos as generating substratum, nature as mediating concealment, and artificial civilization as the highest and most occluding layer.

**Method note:** Folder 36 is one of the clearest places where Dick's writing theory and cosmology collapse into each other. The hidden text is not commentary on reality. It is reality's lower stratum.
