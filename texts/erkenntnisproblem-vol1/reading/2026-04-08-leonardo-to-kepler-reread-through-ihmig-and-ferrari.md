## Main judgment

The reread confirms that the Leonardo to Kepler threshold is best understood as continuity plus mutation at the level of conceptual framework.

Leonardo already earns a strong method line:

- law rather than hidden quality
- analysis rather than raw sensation
- mathematics as the condition for nature's intelligibility
- imagination admitted only under rule

Kepler does not introduce method from nothing. What he changes is the rigor and form of control:

- exact observation now forces theory-revision
- `Erfahrung` gets real content through the Mars discrepancy
- harmony becomes explicit as pure relation
- perception is reconstructed as intellectual formation of spatial order
- hypothesis becomes the opening condition of scientific problem-formation rather than a later computational convenience

So the real transition is not `Leonardo = pre-method` and `Kepler = method`.

It is:

- Leonardo opens the framework of lawful cognition
- Kepler tightens that framework into exact induction, relation-theory, and mathematically guided hypothesis

## 1. Already earned from the primary text

### A. Leonardo already carries more method than the secondary notes add

Parts `80-82` already show that Leonardo is not merely a transitional natural philosopher with some good instincts.

The text itself already gives:

- the dissolution of fixed things into mathematical functions and processes
- the reform of logic through the rights of perception and concept
- the anti-magical break under the thought of necessity
- the reversal from complex appearance back to simple grounds
- the explicit anticipation of Galileo's `resolutive Methode`

So the Ihmig note does not create a structural reading here. It mainly helps state it more cleanly:

- Leonardo matters by a changed framework of lawful cognition, not by isolated discoveries

### B. Kepler already appears as a threshold figure for `Erfahrung`

Parts `83-86` make the change visible without needing outside help.

The primary text already says:

- only with Kepler does the new concept of experience arise
- the eight-minute Mars discrepancy forces reform of astronomy
- objective order remains inseparable from self-active spirit
- harmony is a pure relation, not a thing
- spatial order is constructed through intellect from sensory indications
- mathematical hypothesis is the condition of posing the problem rightly

So the strongest local gains were already in the parts. The secondary notes help sort them.

## 2. What Ihmig strengthens

### A. The Leonardo to Kepler line is best read as continuity plus mutation

Ihmig's value is highest here.

He helps prevent a bad overcorrection in either direction:

- not cumulative accumulation
- not sheer rupture

Applied locally, that means:

- Leonardo and Kepler belong to one methodological line
- but Kepler is not just `more of the same`
- the mutation lies in how experience, relation, and hypothesis are structurally tightened

### B. The real unit of change is the framework of cognition

This is the strongest methodological check from Ihmig.

The question across Parts `80-86` is not:

- which thinker says more advanced things about nature?

It is:

- what form of lawful cognition becomes possible here?

That sharpens the local contrast:

- Leonardo gives law, analysis, and disciplined imagination
- Kepler gives strict observational control, relation as constitutive, and mathematically guided hypothesis as the form of inquiry itself

### C. Kepler's gain is not just better data, but a sharper conceptual regime

The Mars discrepancy matters because it is not only a new fact.

Its role is structural:

- a minimal deviation becomes sufficient to reorganize the whole theory
- observation now controls thought strongly enough to force conceptual revision
- this is the clearest local case of mutation inside continuity

## 3. What Ferrari strengthens

### A. The Kepler pages should not be read as naive empiricism

Ferrari's field-placement note matters here mostly as a guardrail.

The Kepler run already refuses a crude split between observation and constitutive activity:

- thought is checked by observation
- but observation alone does not generate order
- relation, harmony, and spatial form still depend on acts of mind

That means the live text is already inside the same kind of problem Ferrari tracks later:

- how to preserve constitutive activity without falling back into fixed dogma

### B. The best pressure point is the status of relation

Ferrari helps make visible why these Kepler pages matter beyond local history.

Across Parts `84-86`:

- harmony is pure relation
- archetypes are relations, not things
- geometry outranks bare number because it carries form, order, and intelligible structure
- empirical cognition extracts mathematically graspable relations from the given

This is the strongest local reason not to flatten Kepler into either mysticism or empiricism.

### C. Ferrari remains background here, not chapter-governing

The field-placement gain is real, but indirect.

Use it to prevent bad binaries:

- Cassirer versus empiricism
- observation versus spirit
- hypothesis versus truth

Do not use it to force Schlick or Reichenbach into the Kepler chapter itself.

## 4. What changes for the live Kepler work

### A. The contrast with Leonardo needs to be redrawn more precisely

The reread changes one local emphasis.

It is too weak to say:

- Leonardo is still mixed, then Kepler finally becomes scientific

The better line is:

- Leonardo already establishes a framework of lawful, mathematical, anti-magical inquiry
- Kepler makes that framework exact through control, relation, and hypothesis

### B. `Erfahrung` now needs to be watched together with `Hypothese`

The Kepler gain is not only that experience becomes real.

It is also that experience becomes real only within a rationally shaped problem-field:

- mathematical lawfulness is presupposed from the start
- hypothesis is the initial handle by which the problem becomes thinkable
- empirical truth requires integration into a wider mathematically-physical system

This should stay active in the next tranches.

### C. The perception line is no longer secondary

Parts `84-86` show that the harmony discussion is not a decorative detour.

It is the route by which Cassirer moves from:

- exact induction

to:

- relation
- construction of spatial order
- the intellectual character of empirical judgment

So the perception pages should now be read as method-bearing, not as an appendix to Kepler's cosmology.
