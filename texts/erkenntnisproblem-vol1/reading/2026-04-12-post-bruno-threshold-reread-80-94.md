## Main judgment

The right unit is now `80-94`, not `80-83` and no longer merely `80-87`.

The threshold into exact science does not close with the first Kepler appearance or even with the hypothesis sequence. It closes only when Kepler's line has carried:

- exact observation
- harmony as pure relation
- perception as construction
- hypothesis as rational beginning
- force as function
- gravitation as reciprocal interaction
- matter as mass / inertia
- geometry as criterion of scientific possibility
- law as constancy through variation

into one coherent concept of lawful becoming.

That is what Part `94` completes. Part `95` then genuinely begins another unit by handing off to Galilei.

The reread also clarifies the shape of the post-Bruno answer.

Bruno had named mathematics as model and middle without making it operative as a theory of experience. The `80-94` arc shows what that missing operation actually consists in:

- concept does not dominate experience from outside
- experience does not dominate concept from below
- exact science arises when experience becomes usable only through mathematically formed relations, hypotheses, laws, and functions

The positive threshold is therefore not just `more mathematics`.
It is a reconstitution of objectivity.

## 1. Why `80-94` is the right bounded unit

### A. Leonardo alone is opening, not closure

Parts `80-82` already state the positive program with unusual clarity:

- fixed things are dissolved into mathematical functions and processes
- the appeal from words to things culminates in a reform of logic
- mathematics becomes criterion of law, necessity, and anti-magical causal analysis
- experiment is the opening problem, not the final tribunal
- imagination is retained only under rule

That is enough to open the new field, but not enough to complete it.

Leonardo establishes the framework of lawful cognition. He does not yet show its full object-logic.

### B. Early Kepler deepens the framework, but does not yet finish it

Parts `83-87` make several decisive moves:

- `Erfahrung` gains strict content through the Mars discrepancy
- harmony becomes a pure relation rather than a thing
- perception is reconstructed as intellectual formation of spatial order
- hypothesis becomes the rational beginning of empirical science
- mathematics is separated from mere calculation and mystical number-play

This already justifies the earlier `Leonardo -> Kepler` reread note. But by itself it still describes a method regime more than a full conceptual world.

### C. The real completion is the force / law sequence

Parts `88-94` show why the unit has to run further.

The anti-mystical and anti-substantialist polemic is not the end of the Kepler line. It becomes:

- force cut loose from soul through mathematical dependence
- cause redefined as a set of mathematically determinable conditions
- gravitation reformulated as reciprocal interaction
- matter rebuilt through mass, inertia, and quantitative resistance
- geometry made criterion of scientific possibility
- law relocated from fixed figure to rule-governed variation
- cause finally redirected from metaphysical being toward lawful becoming

Only here does the post-Bruno threshold show what a mathematical theory of empirical nature actually looks like.

## 2. What the reread sharpened in the text

### A. Leonardo is stronger than threshold-language alone suggests

The earlier `80-86` reread was right: Leonardo is not just a pre-scientific transitional case.

This broader reread makes the point firmer.

Leonardo already supplies:

- the anti-magical break
- the law / necessity criterion
- the inversion from nobility of object to degree of proof
- the analytical reversal from effect to grounds
- the disciplined relation of imagination to mathematics

So the real difference from Kepler is not that Leonardo lacks method.

It is that Leonardo opens the framework, whereas Kepler forces it into stricter object-formation.

### B. Kepler is the first stable union of control and productivity

The stronger local reading rule now looks like this:

- Kepler is the first figure in whom exact observation and productive inner form stop looking like competing claims
- the mind does not impose arbitrary order on data
- observation does not simply dictate order to a passive mind
- harmony, geometry, relation, and hypothesis articulate a regime in which objectivity is both formed and controlled

That is why Kepler can remain anti-empiricist without becoming anti-experiential.

### C. The real Kepler climax is not the ellipse alone but lawful variation

The ellipse remains the visible emblem, but Parts `93-94` show the deeper point.

Constancy is no longer sought in:

- fixed shape
- circular perfection
- metaphysical form

It is sought in:

- force-principles
- functional law
- lawful variation

This is the clearest local place where exact science ceases to mean preservation of ideal figure and becomes rule-governed becoming.

### D. Force and matter are rebuilt together

The middle Kepler sequence is stronger on this reread than on the first pass.

Force is not added as one more scientific topic. Matter is not a leftover substrate waiting to be measured.

Instead:

- force becomes function
- matter becomes mass, inertia, and resistance
- both become correlates inside one mathematical causality

This is one of the cleanest answers the chapter gives to the old `Substanz -> Funktion` pressure.
