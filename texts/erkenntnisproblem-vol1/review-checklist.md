# Review Checklist

Use this checklist for periodic maintenance passes and for higher-level review of the current state of `erkenntnisproblem-vol1`.

## Per Tranche

- Verify the corrected German against the PDF for the active tranche only.
- Check paragraph boundaries, footnotes, and any mid-sentence carryover at the page break.
- Update `journal.md` with `What this tranche clarified` and `Decision for now`.
- Add or revise glossary entries only when a term is doing repeated structural work.
- Keep source-bound notes in `journal.md`; put comparative or project-side extrapolations in `interpretive-notes.md`.

## Periodic Maintenance

- Harmonize earlier parts after a major chapter arc closes or once a vocabulary cluster has clearly stabilized.
- Re-read the introduction and the current arc for terms under pressure, especially `Geist`, `Wissenschaft`, `Vorstellung`, and any faculty-language that has become technically loaded.
- Strengthen thin glossary notes so each entry says what the term does philosophically, not only which English options are available.
- Verify viewer and PDF stitching across part boundaries where sentences or paragraphs span tranches.
- Regenerate `source/viewer-index.json` after translation, metadata, or export-logic edits that affect the reader.

## Quality Questions

- Does the English preserve the argumentative structure without mechanically copying German period length?
- Is the register right for the local material: methodological introduction, historical exposition, metaphysical argument, or technical psychology?
- Are Latin and historical technical terms kept visible where they are load-bearing?
- Do the part notes summarize what the tranche clarified, not merely what pages it covered?
- Do interpretive notes remain exploratory rather than silently hardening into source-binding claims?
