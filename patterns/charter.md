# Project Charter

`bildung-2.0` is a reconstruction project for whole knowing.

## Problem

The post-war Anglosphere inherited institutional power while losing contact with the deeper traditions that shaped the question of what a human being is for. *Bildung* was replaced by credentialing, specialization, and institutional forgetting. Important texts were not merely neglected but buried, mistranslated, undertranslated, or rendered inaccessible. Knowledge was fragmented into disciplines that no longer speak to one another.

## Aim

Rebuild a broken transmission chain: recover buried texts, reconnect severed lineages of thought, and cultivate a practice of reading, translation, commentary, and synthesis that forms the reader rather than merely informs them.

Not nostalgia. Not anti-modernism. Not academic neutralism. Recovery with discrimination, synthesis with rigor, formation without illusion.

## Subprojects

Each subproject is an organ of one system, not a standalone effort.

| Project | Author | What it trains |
|---------|--------|----------------|
| `texts/escolios/` | Nicolás Gómez Dávila | Semantic doubleness — the distance between close and free translation is understanding, not error |
| `texts/zeitmauer/` | Ernst Jünger | Conceptual patience — major terms stay open until the source resists premature carving |
| `texts/exegesis/` | Philip K. Dick | Recursive mapping — thread dossiers convert a spiraling archive into something navigable without pretending the recursion is noise |
| `sources/` | (triggered by the above) | Original-language encounter — formation tied to small acts of contact with source languages |

## Method

- **Translation as transmission** — not secondary output but one of the main mechanisms of recovery
- **Commentary as equal in dignity to translation** — journals, glossaries, and cross-project notes preserve how understanding develops
- **Cross-domain synthesis** — connecting texts that institutions keep apart, tracing buried genealogies, making structural echoes visible
- **Deferred structure** — terms held `open` or `tentative` until evidence settles them; premature resolution is data loss

## Layers

1. `00-Notes/` — living intellectual workspace: chats, synthesis notes, exploratory writing
2. `patterns/` — crystallized architectural decisions: charter, object types, relations, conventions
3. Subproject directories — the actual translation, commentary, and mapping work
4. (Future) Dynamic view layer — the instrument that lets layers speak to each other

## Limits

The tradition is not *in* books (Schuon). Books are traces of something transmitted through living practice. Textual recovery is necessary but not sufficient. The project should support formation, not merely information, and should hold this limit honestly visible.

## Working Motto

Recover what was buried. Reconnect what was severed. Build forms that can change the reader.
