## Why This Tranche

This is where Peirce's triad becomes architectonic. The movement is not just
"three categories" as a classification scheme. It is an attempt to use first,
second, and third as building materials for a philosophy of chance, law,
habit-taking, mind, matter, and evolution.

## What The Arc Clarified

### 1. The triad carries increasing metaphysical risk

`027` states the wager in compressed form: chance, law, and habit-taking are
the three active elements. It also repeats the anti-axiom pressure from
`Design and Chance`: laws may not be exact, and their present regularity may
itself be a historical product.

Project pressure:

- categories become dangerous when they stop being observed constraints and
  start pretending to be a master key
- the note layer can record the triad as Peirce's wager without adopting it as
  the repo's ontology

### 2. Firstness, secondness, thirdness are not merely numbered slots

In `028`, firstness names immediacy, freshness, spontaneity, and quality before
analysis. Secondness names collision, resistance, hard fact, and brute
thisness. Thirdness names mediation, relation-making, generality, law, and
habit.

The strongest local clarification is negative: triads cannot be reduced to
dyads without losing the mediating fact. By contrast, higher pluralities can be
analyzed through triadic relations. That is why thirdness bears so much weight
in the architecture.

Project pressure:

- an encounter note should distinguish quality, resistance, and mediation
  without forcing them into one object type
- a relation that only looks like an association may need an index, a test, or
  a mediating dossier before it becomes structurally real
- the repo should resist turning "mediation" into a catchall term

### 3. Degeneracy is methodologically useful

`029` makes the categories more usable by naming degenerate seconds and
thirds. The sign discussion aligns with `025`: conventional signs, forceful
indices, and likenesses each do different work. Its consciousness passage also
maps feeling, action/reaction, and synthetic awareness onto first, second, and
third.

Project pressure:

- weak or degenerate relations are not failures; they help classify what kind
  of support a connection actually has
- a visual, diagram, or performance can be a source of relation-making without
  being a propositional argument
- [visual candidate] a small map of token/index/icon against
  first/second/third could be useful later, but not yet as architecture

### 4. Architecture requires material survey before system building

`030` is the methodological safeguard for the whole arc. Peirce criticizes
one-idea philosophies and argues that a philosophical system should be built
only after surveying the available conceptions across the sciences and asking
where each is fit for use.

The mature claim is that philosophy requires thoroughgoing evolutionism:
regularity, law, and matter themselves must be understood as products of
growth. The famous objective-idealist formula belongs here as a speculative
result of that architecture, not as a premise the repo should import.

Project pressure:

- do not build a house out of one good idea
- before promoting a term, ask what material range it has actually survived
- a theory that explains everything too quickly is probably skipping the
  survey of materials

## Decision

No promotion to `texts/` is earned. The usable contribution is a warning about
system-building: triadic thinking can help classify source pressures, but only
while each relation remains traceable to evidence and local use.
