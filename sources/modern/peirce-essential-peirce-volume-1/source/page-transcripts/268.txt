attracting bodies. Why do they attract inversely as the square of the distance? This may be only their average law of attraction; we see how a comet throws away its repulsive material as it approaches the sun. But in the long run, matter that attracts inversely to a higher power of the distance tends perhaps to aggregate itself together, so that the masses of planets which have long been separate tend to attract in this manner.
