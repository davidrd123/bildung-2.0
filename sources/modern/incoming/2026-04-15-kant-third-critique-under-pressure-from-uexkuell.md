## What is actually under pressure

The live question is not `read Kant because Kant is important`.

The live question is narrower:

- Uexkuell's `Planmaessigkeit / Zweckmaessigkeit / Fuegung` cluster is already resisting both mechanism and naive teleology inside the source campaign itself.
- encounter 04 names `Planmaessigkeit` as a `Naturkraft`, one that `nach Regeln bindet` and that our apperception can follow only by binding particulars into a whole
- encounter 02b blocks the empathic shortcut into the animal's inner life and insists on the unity of appearances through our own apperception
- the glossary already warns that `Planmaessigkeit` should not be flattened into subjective intention or doctrinal teleology

That pressure points more directly toward the third Critique's teleological-judgment problem than toward the first Critique's general architecture.

## Source-near surfaces already on disk

### 1. `Planmaessigkeit` is already a local problem of lawful purposive order

The glossary note on `term:planmaessigkeit` already says:

- it works better as `planfulness` or `built according to plan` than as stronger abstraction
- encounter 03 ties it to the lawfulness binding subject and `Umwelt` against mere causality
- encounter 04 names it as a rule-binding `Naturkraft`
- it should not be flattened into subjective intention or naive teleology

That is already close to the teleological-judgment problem before any Kant reading begins.

### 2. Encounter 04 names the pressure in Kant-neighboring vocabulary

The corrected German in encounter 04 says:

- `Diese Naturkraft haben wir Planmaessigkeit genannt`
- we can follow it with our apperception only when we bind many particulars into a whole by rules
- without recognizing this rule-binding natural force, biology remains empty delusion

The normalized source tranche also uses `Zweckmaessigkeit` directly. The pressure is not only retrospective genealogy. The source page is already carrying the term.

### 3. Encounter 02b sharpens the methodological side

The anti-psychologism passage in 02b matters because it says:

- we treat the animal's functional world as our experience, not the animal's experience
- switching into the animal's soul would destroy the coherence of appearances
- the unity of appearances rests on the unity of our own apperception

That does not by itself prove a Kant inheritance. It does show that the live problem is not generic organism-talk. It is a specific pressure around how purposive organization can be apprehended without collapsing either into mechanism or into projected inner life.

## 2026-04-15 — bounded read confirmation

A later bounded read of the localized scaffold confirmed the basic narrowing of
this note without widening it into a Kant campaign.

Read surface:

- `§§61-68` on objective purposiveness, natural purposes, organized beings, and
  teleological judgment
- `§§69-71` on the antinomy and its resolution at the level of reflective
  judgment
- `§77` on discursive understanding and the special need for teleological
  judgment
- `§78` on the practical reconciliation of mechanism and teleology

### What the read confirmed

- The third-Critique pressure is real and more source-near than the
  first-Critique side for current Uexkuell work.
- `§§64-68` are the live contact surface for the current
  `Planmaessigkeit / Fuegung / Funktionskreis` pressure.
- The strongest payoff is methodological, not genealogical: the Uexkuell
  glossary is already holding terms open in a way structurally close to
  reflective-judgment discipline, especially where it refuses both mechanism
  and naive teleology.
- The anti-psychologism / apperception passage in encounter `02b` remains a
  real contact point with this Kant surface, but only as a bounded pressure,
  not as proof of inheritance.

### What the read sharpened

- `Funktionskreis` is the most testable point, because it may extend the
  reciprocal-causation structure of the organized being beyond the organism
  alone into the organism-world circuit.
- `Planmaessigkeit` should continue to be held as an open problem of lawful
  purposive order, not flattened either into subjective intention or into
  doctrinal teleology.
- `§77` is especially dangerous for retrofit genealogy. It explains why
  teleological judgment becomes necessary for our kind of understanding, but it
  should not be promoted into a formation chain or broader German-idealist
  architecture here.
