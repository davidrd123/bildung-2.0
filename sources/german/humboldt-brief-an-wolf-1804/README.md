# Humboldt - Brief an F. A. Wolf vom 16. Juni 1804

Wilhelm von Humboldt, letter to Friedrich August Wolf, Rome, 16 June 1804.
Printed in *Wilhelm von Humboldt's gesammelte Werke*, Band V. Berlin:
G. Reimer, 1846, pp. 264-267.

## What This Is

A small German source-language landing zone for the Wolf letter cited by
Cassirer in PSF I p. 98 and in *Die Kantischen Elemente in Wilhelm von
Humboldts Sprachphilosophie*.

This is not a general Humboldt correspondence shelf. It exists because the
letter is the compact chapter I opening support for Cassirer's Humboldt
section, especially the line in which Humboldt describes his own work as
`Sprachstudium`.

## Current Finding

The letter is printed as no. `LXV`, `Rom, den 16. Junius, 1804`, pp. 264-267.
The IA text layer misreads the last date digit as `%`; a rendered page-image
check confirms `1804`.

Cassirer's cited locus sits on printed pp. 266-267. The working normalized
excerpt is enough for source-near comparison, but the full raw letter remains
available for context.
