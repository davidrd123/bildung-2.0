## 2026-04-02 — Initial scaffold

**Why this campaign exists**

Anders is already doing real work in the repo's genealogy, especially where the apparatus-world needs a bridge between Cassirer's symbolic-form history and Juenger's phenomenology of the technological threshold.

Band I is now present locally in a clean EPUB-derived TXT, which means the first pass can begin as source work rather than file rescue.

**Decision for now**

Keep this under `sources/` as a bounded campaign with its own line map, glossary, and journal.

That gives the work enough structure to accumulate pressure without pretending that a full Anders translation project has already been earned.

- whether `prometheische Scham` and `prometheisches Gefälle` create sustained glossary pressure rather than one-off citation value
- whether the apparatus-world line (`Vorentscheidung`, `Weltphantom`, `Phantom` / `Matrize`) needs more than encounter-scale treatment
- whether the bomb line (`Apokalypseblindheit`) remains part of one coherent campaign or starts demanding its own larger lane

**What the first pass should clarify**

- how much of Anders's active vocabulary should stay German in the running draft
- whether the essay form is best handled as bounded anchor passages rather than a sequential march
- how tightly the Anders line should remain coupled to live Junger and Cassirer work
