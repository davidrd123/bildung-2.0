## 2026-04-02 — Encounter 01 worked

**What this tranche clarified**

- The late preface is not optional framing. It binds the bomb, media, and Beckett lines together retrospectively as one diagnosis.
- `Vorentscheidung` is real pressure, not rhetoric. Devices are denied the status of means and recast as decisions already made over us.
- `Weltphantom` is stronger than image-language. The problem is a phantomized access to world, not merely false pictures.
- `prometheisches Gefaelle` is the first major load-bearing term of the campaign. It spreads across making, imagining, feeling, responsibility, and embodiment.
- Anders's methodological `Uebertreibung` is part of the claim. Understatement would itself reproduce the epoch's minimization of the object.

**Decision for now**

- Let `Vorentscheidung` stand provisionally as `predecision`.
- Keep `Weltphantom` and `prometheisches Gefaelle` visibly German in the running draft.
- Keep `Apokalypseblindheit` open until the bomb opening is worked directly.

**Implication**

The Anders line begins more tightly unified than it first looked. The media argument, the anthropological argument, and the bomb argument are already tied together in the opening tranche.

**Review notes**

- [visual candidate] apparatus as `Vorentscheidung`: local choices nested inside one macro-device world

## 2026-04-02 — Encounter 02 worked

**What this tranche clarified**

- `prometheische Scham` is not generic technophobia and not simply shame before machines. It is shame at being born rather than made, shame before the exactness and superior made-ness of one's own products.
- The old Promethean posture turns dialectically. Pride in self-making flips into inferiority before the manufactured thing.
- The first objection is decisive: for most people the device-world is not lived as "our" achievement, but as an alien commodity world.
- Shame's invisibility is structural. It hides itself through visible shamelessness.
- The `Verdinglichung` passage is harsher than a standard critique of reification: the human being comes to feel not-being-thing as lack.

**Decision for now**

- Keep `prometheische Scham` open and visibly German in the running draft.
- Treat `Promethean shame` only as an orienting gloss.
- Keep `prometheischer Stolz`, `prometheischer Trotz`, `Verdinglichung`, and `Dingscham` local to the encounter for now.

**Implication**

The campaign's first two encounters are already teaching one methodological lesson: Anders's hardest losses happen where English looks almost effortless. The danger is not opacity but false transparency.

**Review notes**

- [visual candidate] the Promethean reversal: pride -> inferiority -> self-reification

## 2026-04-02 — Encounter 04 worked

**What this tranche clarified**

- The Beckett essay is not a decorative detour. It supplies a phenomenology of the same worldlessness and passivity already visible in the media and shame encounters.
- `Sein ohne Zeit` names a structural condition rather than a striking title.
- `Nicht-Welt` and `Weltlosigkeit` are stronger than mood words; they name the loss of world as a field of action and collision.
- The waiting analysis matters because it shows bare persistence misreading itself as expectation.

**Decision for now**

- Keep `Sein ohne Zeit` open and visibly German in the running draft.
- Keep `Nicht-Welt` and `Weltlosigkeit` local for now.
- Let `Massenmensch` continue to gather pressure across encounters.

**Implication**

The Anders line is not splitting into separate themes. Shame, media, and worldlessness are proving to be different surfaces of one diagnosis.

**Review notes**

- [visual candidate] the Beckett structure: `Nicht-Welt` -> bare persistence -> waiting misread as hope

## 2026-04-02 — Encounter 05 worked

**What this tranche clarified**

- `Apokalypseblindheit` is not an ornament of the bomb essay. It names a central incapacity of the epoch.
- Anders's overstatement is methodological and ethical at once. Understatement would reproduce the object's own social minimization.
- The decisive shift is from the `Wie`-question to the `Ob`-question.
- Humanity is no longer merely mortal; it has become collectively killable.
- The Titan image is immediately doubled by incapacity. Omnipotence and disproportion appear together.

**Decision for now**

- Keep `Apokalypseblindheit` open and visibly German in the running draft.
- Keep `potestas annihilationis`, `Titanen`, and `toetbar` local to the encounter for now.
- Treat the `Ob`-question as one of the campaign's major earned formulations.

**Implication**

The first Anders queue closes as one arc: shame, media, worldlessness, and apocalypse are four expressions of the same disproportion between human capacities and self-made worlds.

**Review notes**

- [visual candidate] the `Wie` -> `Ob` shift under the sign of the bomb

## 2026-04-02 — Queue assessment after five encounters

**Decision**

The initial Anders queue has earned completion as a bounded source campaign, but not promotion to `texts/`.

**Why it stays bounded**

- the glossary is still lean enough for a `sources/` campaign
- the work has generated term pressure, not yet a sequential whole-book lane
- the journal drift is still source-bound rather than architecturally wider than the campaign itself
- the clean TXT source makes line-anchored encounter work sufficient for now

**What the queue established**

- `Vorentscheidung` can stand provisionally as `predecision`
- `prometheische Scham`, `prometheisches Gefaelle`, `Weltphantom`, `Phantom / Matrize`, `Sein ohne Zeit`, `Massenmensch`, and `Apokalypseblindheit` all remain genuinely open
- the Beckett and bomb essays do not sit outside the main line; they intensify it
- the bounded source-campaign form can hold Anders's conceptual density without needing immediate promotion

**What has not yet been earned**

- no full `texts/` promotion
- no local raw/normalized tranche workflow by default
- no standing cross-project dossier beyond the current source-bound notes

**Implication**

The first five encounters confirm Anders as a major live line in the repo, but still as a bounded source campaign rather than a standing translation project.
