## Why This Tranche

This is where the apparatus-world becomes media-theoretical without becoming merely media-theoretical.

The opening sections already concentrate the decisive moves:

- no medium is merely a means
- the world is delivered into the house in phantom form
- mass consumption becomes solitary
- the consumer becomes an unpaid homeworker in the production of the `Massenmensch`

## Current Pressure

The key problem here is architectural. `Phantom`, `Matrize`, and `Weltphantom` appear to form one machine, and flattening any one of them too early will probably damage the others.

This tranche will also test whether Anders's media vocabulary belongs inside the same bounded campaign as `prometheische Scham`, or whether it starts trying to become its own independent line.
