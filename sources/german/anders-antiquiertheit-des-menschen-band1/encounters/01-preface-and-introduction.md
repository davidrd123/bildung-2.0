## Scope

- Source: `../../../books/Die Antiquiertheit des Menschen - Gunther Anders.txt`

## Why This Tranche

This is where Anders states the campaign's major lines before the essays disperse them:

- the three theses from the late preface
- the apparatus-world as already-decided milieu
- `Weltphantom` as a condition rather than a media side effect
- `prometheisches Gefälle` as the structural spread between what we make and what we can imagine, feel, and answer for
- overstatement as a truth-practice for a systematically minimized object

## Current Pressure

The main difficulty here is not one isolated term but Anders's whole opening posture. Devices are denied the status of mere means and recast as `Vorentscheidungen`; the media argument already reaches toward `Weltphantom`; the methodological remarks on exaggeration and Gelegenheitsphilosophie are part of the claim, not decorative framing.

This tranche will set the tonal discipline for the rest of the campaign. If it is read too fluently, later terms will look like detachable slogans rather than parts of one diagnosis.

## Terms to Watch
