## Key decisions made

1. **Commentary is first-class.** Thread dossiers sit at equal dignity to translation, not as footnotes.
2. **The texts teach the method.** Enshrined in the charter with specific examples from all three texts.
3. **Review discipline.** Cross-batch observations must be written to the journal, not left in conversation. Visual candidates tagged with `[visual candidate]`.
4. **Handle schema adopted.** Phase 1 (convention) is live in glossary and journal. Handles used in viewer.
5. **Deferred structure is philosophical, not just practical.** §§8, 27, 34, 48, 51 all provide textual grounding for why the glossary holds terms open.
6. **The translation is recovering a position, not just a text.** No Anglo equivalent combines Jünger's elements (argument-through-image, textile temporal model, Hinzutretende, naming crisis, time wall as present threshold).

## Key findings from the translation

### Method findings (texts teach the method)

- §8: measurable knowledge destroys what it measures → deferred structure
- §11: formative power, not truth, is the gain → Bildung over information
- §20: tragedy weaves fateful time; in measurable time there are only accidents
- §25: the plan fears the musische — art, song, immediate perception
- §27: truth may "become true" if inner optics change → deferred structure as readiness
- §31: So-Sein resists Gleichsein through form, not argument
- §34: facts create evidence, not truths → handles create evidence, not understanding
- §36: pure comparison creates relations, not standards → limit of the handle system
- §41: mechanical counting misses qualitative transitions → vertebrae warning
- §48: the time wall named; naming itself becomes dangerous → why glossary holds terms open
- §51: future things arrive before their names do
- §54: facts don't change; they indicate changes

### Structural patterns

- **The textile thread** (warp/weft): §§6/14/20/22/25/28 — measurable time as warp, fateful time as weft
- **"They follow one another but no longer resemble one another"**: §§9/13/41 — Jünger's recurring definition of qualitative time
- **The widening spiral of Part 2**: seven concentric rings applying the measurable/fateful distinction to astrology → planning → science → tragedy → happiness → periodicity → So-Sein
- **Part 3's two-phase structure**: diagnosis (§§32-43: limits of morphology) → consequence (§§44-55: what happens when historical categories fail)
- **Three-thread convergence at §§35-37**: time-crisis, goethe-leibniz-frame, and mumford-bridge converge on the question of what supplies the measure pure comparison cannot
