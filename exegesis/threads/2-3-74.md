# 2-3-74: The Originating Experience

The shorthand Dick uses for the visions and experiences of February–March 1974 that generated the entire Exegesis. Everything in the archive is an attempt to decode this.

## What Happened (composite from early entries and letters)

- Break-in at San Rafael home, November 1971 — the dark precursor
- Experiments with megadose vitamins and neural disinhibition
- Phosphene graphics resembling Kandinsky/Klee/Picasso, lasting 8 hours
- "Pink beam" / gold and red plasmatic entity arranging reality
- Sense of a second or antique intelligence overlaid on his own consciousness; later crystallized as Thomas
- Dream print-outs: words, sentences, galley proofs, a "huge book"
- Diagnosed his infant son Christopher's hernia
- Experienced Rome c. 70 A.D. superimposed on 1974 California

- `[26:45]` 3-74 is reframed as a transformation of being, perception, knowledge, and action rather than a detachable set of mystical contents
- `[26:49–50]` Dick reaches maximum certainty about the kind of deity he saw while also stressing the event's practical ineffability
- `[26:52]` the experience is treated as a recording event: Dick becomes a witness-medium in whom a stage of the organism is preserved
- `[27:4–13]` Dick tests the event against paranoia, schizophrenia, and right-brain breakthrough, accepting a therapeutic account without reducing the vision to pathology
- `[27:41–49]` 3-74 becomes a temporary restoration of bicamerality and hidden divine command
- `[27:77]` the experience is described as the answer to the entire 26-year question posed by Dick's fiction
- `[27:102–103]` Dick models the event as retrograde transit to the intervention point and return into a rectified alternate present
- `[28:17]` 3-74 is redescribed as entry into sacred time rather than simple backward time-travel

- `[29:19–23]` the event is reinterpreted as direct colloquy with the world soul itself rather than a merely local encounter
- `[29:31–46]` Dick insists that the revelation's core truth is Christ's substitution within undeserved suffering and practical intervention rather than explanation
