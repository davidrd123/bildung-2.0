# Encounter: Lotman's random-becomes-determined ~ Mooney's karmic disposition as perspective-preserver

Two passages from different disciplinary vocabularies naming the same structural moment: the bifurcation point where openness crystallizes into lawfulness via a preserving mechanism. One written as historical semiotics; the other as analytic metaphysics of personal identity. Placed on the bench to test whether the structural homology is real enough to recruit further material, and to test whether the bench handles philosophical primary-text passages as well as literary ones.

## Passages

**Lotman, *Внутри мыслящих миров*, Part III ch. 2 (*Исторические закономерности и структура текста*):**

> Таким образом, случайное и закономерное перестают быть несовместимыми понятиями, а предстают как два возможных состояния одного и того же объекта. Двигаясь в детерминированном поле, он предстает точкой в линейном развитии, попадая в флуктуационное пространство — выступает как континуум потенциальных возможностей со случайностью в качестве пускового устройства.

> [...] Случайный до реализации, выбор становится детерминированным после. Ретроспективность усиливает детерминированность.

Literal: *Thus, the random and the lawful cease to be incompatible concepts, and appear as two possible states of the same object. Moving in a determined field, it appears as a point in linear development; entering a fluctuational space, it comes forward as a continuum of potential possibilities with chance as the triggering device. [...] Random before realization, the choice becomes determined after. Retrospectivity strengthens determinacy.*

**Mooney, *Reincarnation for Everyone* (forthcoming, *Mind*), p. 11:**

> However we tell the story, one might wonder what it is about the karmic dispositions of the dying body that enables them to determine which particular first-person perspective the new body supports without affecting that body or the person it begins to constitute in any other way. The answer lies in the fact that the karmic disposition in question is a disposition to preserve whichever first-person perspective is already supported by the body in which it resides. So given that my body supports my first-person perspective, if the karmic disposition is one of the causes of the fact that the new body begins to support some first-person perspective, then it will inevitably be my first-person perspective.

## Field note (2026-04-19)

Both passages describe the same structural moment: a bifurcation point at which a system (cultural semiosphere for Lotman; constituted human person for Mooney) passes from open-field to determinate-trajectory, and they do so via a mechanism that *preserves* prior determinations without collapsing the prior openness. Lotman leaves the mechanism formal — chance as triggering device, with thinking-being intervention sharpening the gradation. Mooney names the mechanism causally: karmic disposition as a preserving-operator for whichever first-person perspective was being supported prior to the bifurcation (biological death).

The homology is specific, not just atmospheric. Lotman's "random before realization, determined after" describes exactly what Mooney's karmic disposition *does*: at death, which perspective will install in some developing body is open; after the karmic operator fires, it is retrospectively determined that this body always was going to install this perspective. The operator is a **retrospective-determination mechanism named at ontogenetic scale**. Mooney has given a precise philosophical vocabulary for what Lotman theorizes as formal semiospheric structure.

The vocabulary gap is informative in both directions. Lotman's vocabulary extends the operator's scale — it is not only what karma does at birth-to-birth transitions, but what any bifurcation-with-thinking-being does, up to civilizational scale. Mooney's vocabulary sharpens the ontogenetic case — karmic disposition as trope-instance, as causal power, potentially instantiable in material substrates. The two vocabularies illuminate each other in ways that neither reaches alone.

The second Mooney move to notice: the multiverse hypotheses (Lewis modal, Hudson hyperspatial) function as **preservation-of-the-open-field operators**. If all possibility-branches are realized somewhere, the untaken branch is not foreclosed; it is realized elsewhere. This is what literature does at textual scale (per earlier conversation: art holds bifurcation open inside a culture that needs closure to function) performed by analytic metaphysics at ontological scale. Mooney may not intend this structural parallel; it is nonetheless present.

Not synthesizing further. The pair is on the bench.
