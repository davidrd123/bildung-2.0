2. `grounds` — the `Gegenstand / Objekt` distinction should not be collapsed

* **Erk source:** `journal.md`, slice `2020-2199` (Leonardo / Kepler threshold), especially the claim that the object of research arises only through a processing of sensation that dissolves fixed things into mathematical functions and processes; cross-check `reading/2026-04-08-earned-distillation-from-volume-i.md`, sections `2` and `5`.

3. `opens` — `Funktionskreis` should be read under `Funktionsbegriff` pressure, not as a loose life-image

* **Erk source:** `journal.md`, slice `390-449` (explicit `Substanzbegriff -> Funktionsbegriff` turn), plus `reading/2026-04-08-earned-distillation-from-volume-i.md`, sections `2` and `5`.

That does not settle Uexküll’s term, but it opens a sharper test: does `Funktionskreis` merely name a closed organic routine, or does it actually re-cut object, act, and world in a rule-bearing way?

4. `does not reach` — the completed Volume I arc does **not** yet ground Uexküll’s full `Umwelt` / many-worlds claim

* **Erk source:** `reading/2026-04-08-earned-distillation-from-volume-i.md`, sections `1-5`; `reading/2026-04-08-whole-run-parts-journal-ledger-scratch-note.md`, especially `Book I middle`, `Naturphilosophie opening`, and `Exact science threshold`.
