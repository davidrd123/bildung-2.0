Using the project’s own threshold — promote only when a term does repeated structural work — and the close-review note that treats the journal, ledger, and page-map as trustworthy full-volume surfaces, the issue looks more like distribution than raw size: 49 glossary entries, but heavy early-book capture and thin later-book uptake. (review-checklist.md, “Per Tranche,” “Periodic Maintenance”; reading/2026-04-08-volume-i-close-review.md, “Closure state,” “What remains trustworthy”)

**Legend:** **strong case for promotion** = repeated structural work across later stretches; **borderline** = real recurrence, but either chapter-local, English-stable, or more pattern than term; **correctly deferred** = the apparatus noticed it and was right not to carve it.

## 1) Missed promotions from early watch decisions that later recurred without getting glossary entries

* **Relation / Verhältnis / Grundbegriff der Relation — strong case for promotion.**
  This is the clearest miss. The journal explicitly holds `Relation` back in the Leonardo/Kepler method check, then later makes it structurally central in Kepler (harmony as pure relation), Descartes (`Logik der Relationen`), and Malebranche (`Der Grundbegriff der Relation`). The ledger repeats the same pressure in both the Kepler and Malebranche runs, and `source/glossary.yaml` has no home for it. (journal.md, “Leonardo / Kepler method check through Ihmig and Ferrari,” “Thirty-Third Naturphilosophie Tranche,” “Sixty-First Naturphilosophie Tranche,” “Eighty-Ninth” and “Ninetieth Naturphilosophie Tranche”; close-reading-ledger.md, “Kepler: harmony becomes pure relation and opens perception theory,” “Malebranche: force, relativism, and the concept of relation”)

* **Unendliches / the infinite — strong case for promotion.**
  The journal first treats `Unendliches` as a watch item in late *Naturphilosophie*, but it then recurs as a named hinge in Bruno, the mathematics section, Descartes, Pascal, Malebranche, and Bayle. The glossary has chapter-local Latinized infinity formulas (`scientia infinita`) but no generic entry for the recurring volume-level problem. (journal.md, “Seventeenth Naturphilosophie Tranche,” “Twentieth Naturphilosophie Tranche,” “Fifty-Fifth Naturphilosophie Tranche,” “Seventy-Third Naturphilosophie Tranche,” “Seventy-Seventh Naturphilosophie Tranche,” “Ninety-Second Naturphilosophie Tranche,” “Ninety-Seventh Naturphilosophie Tranche”; close-reading-ledger.md, “Campanella: the infinite corrects sensualism from within,” “Bayle: antinomies of the infinite and the fate of theoretical reason”)

* **Kraft — strong case for promotion.**
  `Kraft` is repeatedly watched in Book II, then becomes a major pivot: Bruno’s dynamic nature, Kepler’s separation of force from soul and recasting of force as function, and Malebranche’s critique of force. The apparatus clearly treats it as more than a local word, but it never enters the glossary. (journal.md, “Twenty-Third Naturphilosophie Tranche,” “Thirty-Eighth Naturphilosophie Tranche,” “Thirty-Ninth Naturphilosophie Tranche,” “Fortieth Naturphilosophie Tranche,” “Eighty-Ninth Naturphilosophie Tranche”; close-reading-ledger.md, “Bruno: analysis sharpens, then yields to force and development,” “Kepler: force breaks from soul through mathematical dependence,” “Kepler: force becomes mathematical function and an arithmetic of forces,” “Malebranche: force, relativism, and the concept of relation”)

* **Gesetz / Naturgesetz — strong case for promotion.**
  The journal first keeps `Gesetz` under restraint in the Leonardo stretch, then repeatedly rebuilds whole sections around it: Bruno’s `Natur und Gesetz`, Kepler’s `Der Begriff des Gesetzes` and explicit `Naturgesetz`, Galileo’s law/harmony break, Descartes’ law-governed deductive frame, and Malebranche’s law-centered recoding of efficacy. No glossary entry appears. (journal.md, “Thirtieth Naturphilosophie Tranche,” “Twenty-Fourth Naturphilosophie Tranche,” “Forty-Second,” “Forty-Third,” “Fifty-Third,” “Sixty-Sixth,” and “Ninetieth Naturphilosophie Tranche”; close-reading-ledger.md, “Bruno: substance dissolves into all-nature and rule,” “Kepler: constancy shifts from figure to law and from being to becoming,” “Galilei: idea against purpose, law against harmony,” “Malebranche: relation, divine law, and psychological idealism”)

* **Potenz / Potenzbegriff — strong case for promotion.**
  The project starts by putting `Potenz/Akt` on watch in Bovillus, then reopens it explicitly in *Naturphilosophie* through `Die Kritik des Potenzbegriffs`, and later reuses it in Bruno’s dynamic development line. That is enough recurrence to outrun the original deferral. (journal.md, “Transition to Carolus Bovillus,” “First Full Bovillus Tranche,” “Third Naturphilosophie Tranche,” “Twenty-Third Naturphilosophie Tranche”; close-reading-ledger.md, “Potency critique: boundary concept turned metaphysical essence,” “Bruno: analysis sharpens, then yields to force and development”)

* **Hypothese — borderline.**
  `Hypothese` clearly does real method-work in Kepler and then again in Descartes, but its spread is narrower than relation/law/infinite, and the apparatus never shows the same translation-instability or chapter-to-chapter pressure. It looks under-served, but less decisively than the four cases above. (journal.md, “Thirty-Fifth,” “Thirty-Sixth,” “Thirty-Seventh,” and “Sixty-Eighth Naturphilosophie Tranche”; close-reading-ledger.md, “Kepler: hypothesis is the rational beginning of empirical science,” “Kepler: hypothesis gives astronomy its concept and separates math from mysticism,” “Descartes: possible worlds and physical hypotheses”)

## 2) Recurring ledger constructions or pressure-patterns with no glossary home

* **Reification / `Verdinglichung` of cognitive functions — strong case for promotion.**
  This is not just a stray wording choice. The April 3 distillation already elevates the `Herrschaft`/reification thread to working status, and the ledger later replays it in Telesio and Geulincx/Burthogge as a diagnostic pattern: thought, soul, cognition, and categories keep being turned into thing-like contents. The glossary has no entry for the pattern itself. (journal.md, “2026-04-03 - Reading-note distillation promoted into working status,” “Ninth Naturphilosophie Tranche,” “Eighty-Third,” “Eighty-Fourth,” and “Eighty-Fifth Naturphilosophie Tranche”; close-reading-ledger.md, “Telesio: Aristotelian form survives in psychology,” “Geulincx: teleology, dogmatism, and concept-illusion,” “Geulincx and Burthogge: thing-concept, dualism, and `modus concipiendi`”)

* **`Erkennen -> Messen -> Gleichung -> Beziehen` — borderline.**
  The ledger catches this first in early Cusanus, where knowing is explicitly defined through measuring and equation. It resurfaces later in Descartes, where `Messen` becomes a pure form of `Beziehen`, and in the mathematics/exact-science run as common measure of relations. But this is a construction-family more than a single term, so it feels like a real homeless pressure rather than an obvious missing glossary headword. (close-reading-ledger.md, “Cusanus opening: cognition defined as measuring,” “Descartes: analytic geometry and common measure”; journal.md, “Sixty-Second,” “Sixty-Third,” and “Fifty-Eighth Naturphilosophie Tranche”)

* **`thing/substance -> relation/function/law` conversion — borderline.**
  The glossary has partial coverage through `Substanzbegriff` and `Funktionsbegriff`, but the recurrent apparatus pattern is broader: Galileo, Descartes, and Malebranche keep restaging the same conversion from thing-language to relation/law/function. The relational and lawful side of that pattern remains homeless. (journal.md, “Humanism Continued,” “Fiftieth,” “Fifty-First,” “Fifty-Second,” “Sixty-First,” and “Ninetieth Naturphilosophie Tranche”; close-reading-ledger.md, “Galilei: from substance to function,” “Malebranche: relation, divine law, and psychological idealism”)

## 3) Correctly deferred watch terms that never earned promotion

* **Historismus — correctly deferred.**
  The journal explicitly keeps it on watch and then off the glossary. Nothing in the later apparatus turns it into a recurring operative term. (journal.md, “Skepticism Opening and Montaigne,” “Harmonization Pass Through Parts 01-44”)

* **`religio indita` — correctly deferred.**
  The journal first holds it on watch, then explicitly keeps it off the glossary at the end of Section IV. Later stretches do not reactivate it as a structural term. (journal.md, “History, Psychology, and Religious Universality,” “Universal Religion and the Logos Conclusion”)

* **Ataraxie / Subjektivismus — correctly deferred.**
  Both get marked as real watch terms in the Montaigne ethics turn, but they stay local to the skepticism chapter and do not become recurring apparatus-level handles. (journal.md, “Montaigne, Ataraxia, and the Moral Reversal”)

* **`Sichtiges` / `Unsichtiges` — correctly deferred.**
  The journal calls this a strong local watch-point in Paracelsus, but it never grows beyond that local methodological seam. (journal.md, “Fifth Naturphilosophie Tranche”; close-reading-ledger.md, “Paracelsus: `Erfahrung` splits into method and `Irrsaal`”)

* **`Licht der Natur` — correctly deferred.**
  Its double meaning is genuinely important in Paracelsus/Telesio, but the journal keeps treating it as chapter-local rather than volume-bearing, and the later apparatus does not force a reversal of that judgment. (journal.md, “Fifth Naturphilosophie Tranche,” “Sixth Naturphilosophie Tranche”)

* **`subnotio` / `esse ab anima` — correctly deferred.**
  These are real Fracastoro pressure-points, but they do not recur outside that bridge from sensualism to self-consciousness. (journal.md, “Seventh Naturphilosophie Tranche”; close-reading-ledger.md, “Fracastoro: self-consciousness breaks the sensualist schema”)

## 4) Glossary gaps by chapter/stretch

* **Book II, `C) Die Begriffe des Raumes und der Zeit` plus the Bruno run — strong case for promotion.**
  This is the first major under-glossed block. The page-map marks these as sustained subsections, not isolated notes, and the journal keeps watching `reiner Raum`, `Unendliches`, `Kraft`, `Potenz`, `Gesetz`, and `Minimum/Grenze` without stabilizing them. The glossary remains comparatively quiet exactly where the book’s conceptual pressure is rising. (outline.yaml, Book II; page-map.yaml, “begriffe-des-raumes-und-der-zeit-opening-within-naturphilosophie,” “kopernikanisches-weltsystem-und-metaphysik-opening-within-naturphilosophie,” “der-begriff-des-unendlichen-within-bruno,” “natur-und-gesetz-within-bruno,” “der-begriff-des-minimums-within-bruno”; journal.md, Parts 65–79 range)

* **Book II, `Die Entstehung der exakten Wissenschaft` — especially Kepler, Galilei, and `Die Mathematik` — strong case for promotion.**
  This is the second major gap. The page-map names whole subsection chains around harmony, force, law, Galilei, and mathematics; the journal then repeatedly says “still no glossary promotion” while relation, hypothesis, law, force, analysis, measure, infinity, continuum, and symbolic definition do most of the work. (outline.yaml, Book II chapter map; page-map.yaml, “die-entstehung-der-exakten-wissenschaft-opening,” “der-begriff-der-harmonie-within-kepler,” “der-begriff-der-kraft-within-kepler,” “der-begriff-des-gesetzes-within-kepler,” “galilei-section-opening,” “die-mathematik-opening-within-entstehung-der-exakten-wissenschaft”; journal.md, Parts 80–109 range)

* **Book III from `Descartes` through `Logik und Kategorienlehre`, `Die Ideenlehre — Malebranche`, and Bayle — strong case for promotion.**
  The third major gap is the whole later idealism field. The page-map shows long named subsections on relation, order/measure, judgment, eternal truths, idea/perception, category, substance, and causality; the ledger confirms these are repeated structural pivots. Yet the glossary barely grows here. (outline.yaml, Book III; page-map.yaml, “descartes-opening,” “logik-und-kategorienlehre-opening,” “die-ideenlehre-malebranche-opening,” “der-ausgang-der-cartesischen-philosophie-bayle-opening”; journal.md, Parts 110–149 range; close-reading-ledger.md, Descartes/Malebranche/Bayle entries)

* **`Der Skepticismus` — correctly deferred as a non-gap.**
  This chapter is comparatively well judged by restraint. Its important watch terms (`Historismus`, `Ataraxie`, `Subjektivismus`) mostly remain local to its ethical and rhetorical argument, and the later apparatus does not prove that caution wrong. (outline.yaml, Book I; page-map.yaml, “skepticism-chapter-opening,” “montaigne-within-skepticism,” “charron-within-skepticism”; journal.md, Montaigne/Charron/Sanchez entries)

## 5) Over-represented glossary entries that got early treatment but did not carry much weight later

Most of these are only **borderline** over-represented, not mistakes. The real asymmetry is that early local entries got carved while later Book II/III clusters did not.

* **Rechenmarken — borderline.**
  It mattered in the introduction and had real local pressure, but it never became a recurring volume-level handle afterward. (journal.md, “Second Introduction Tranche”; glossary.yaml, entry `Rechenmarken`)

* **Ars oppositorum — borderline.**
  Strong within Bovillus, thin afterward. It looks justified locally but over-weighted relative to later unentered Book II/III pressures. (journal.md, “Transition to Carolus Bovillus”; glossary.yaml, entry `Ars oppositorum`)

* **Comprehension — borderline.**
  The Nizolius tranche rightly marked it as the counter-concept to scholastic abstraction, but it does not carry much later volume-wide load. (journal.md, “Francesco Pico Conclusion and Nizolius Opening”; glossary.yaml, entry `Comprehension`)

* **Cabbala — borderline.**
  It is load-bearing for one Section IV model of historical unity, but it does not continue as a major apparatus term later. (journal.md, “Bovillus and the Opening of Renaissance History-Philosophy”; glossary.yaml, entry `Cabbala`)

* **perennis philosophia — borderline.**
  It clearly earns its Section IV moment, but later recurrence is slight compared with the later unentered exact-science and post-Cartesian clusters. (journal.md, “History, Psychology, and Religious Universality”; glossary.yaml, entry `perennis philosophia`)

The net audit result is: the strongest missed promotions are **relation**, **infinite**, **force**, **law**, and **potency**; the strongest homeless pattern is **reification**; the strongest justified restraints are in the skepticism and local Paracelsus/Fracastoro watch terms.
