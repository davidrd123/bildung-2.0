# Experiment 04: `Merk* / Funktionskreis / Planmaessigkeit` family cluster

Purpose: test whether the Uexkuell source campaign now earns a smallest real
family-level claim stronger than "these terms are related," without collapsing
their distinct work into one doctrine.

Status: living-layer packet result. This is a bounded result note, not a
glossary or architecture decision.

## Question

What is the minimum real family claim the source now earns across
`Merkzeichen`, `Merkmal`, `Merkwelt`, `Umwelt`, `Funktionskreis`, and
`Planmaessigkeit`?

## Packet

See `packet-04-merk-funktionskreis-planmaessigkeit-family-cluster.md`.

## Method

1. Read only the packet's cited source surfaces and the minimum local context
   they already point to.
2. Treat the prior three `Umwelt` experiment notes as bounded carry-forward,
   not as authority above the passages.
3. Ask for the smallest claim that binds the family without turning lexical
   kinship into proof of conceptual unity.
4. Force the result back onto current English tendencies rather than widening
   into a theory of Uexkuell.

## Result

The minimum family claim now earned is not merely that these terms belong near
each other. It is that the source is building one **serial organism-world
articulation** across them.

Encounter 03 defines `Merkzeichen` as the just-noticeable change in attention
and then states that these inner `Merkzeichen` become the world's `Merkmale`.
Encounter 02 defines the sum of `Merkmale` as `Merkwelt`, binds `Merkwelt` and
`Wirkwelt` into `Umwelt`, and then places `Innenwelt` and `Umwelt` together
inside the `Funktionskreis`. Encounter 02b tightens this further by insisting
that the outer segment of the circle must also be read under
`Planmaessigkeit`, not merely under causality.

So the family bond is not shared vocabulary. It is a staged transfer and
embedding:

- inner discriminative sign
- worldly mark
- summed perceptual side
- paired organism-world whole
- full circuit
- lawful or planful reading of that whole

That is the smallest real claim this packet earns.

## What binds the family

**The family is bound by one continuous passage from inner differentiation to
worldly articulation, and then by one continuous enclosure of that articulation
inside the functional circle under `Planmaessigkeit`.**

More exactly:

- `Merkzeichen` names the inner unit of discriminative uptake.
- `Merkmal` names what that same structure becomes on the world side.
- `Merkwelt` gathers those worldly `Merkmale` as one side of the animal's
  world.
- `Umwelt` is not the starting noun for surroundings, but the result of binding
  `Merkwelt` and `Wirkwelt` together.
- `Funktionskreis` is the larger circuit that entwines `Innenwelt` and
  `Umwelt`.
- `Planmaessigkeit` names how this whole must be interpreted once both the
  bodily and outer-world segments are in view.

This is why the cluster is real: each term occupies a distinct place in one
serial construction, and the passages explicitly move through those places.

## What still differentiates the family members

The family does not collapse into one generic doctrine because the terms do
different work at different levels.

- `Merkzeichen` is not a world-term. It names the thresholded inner change of
  attention.
- `Merkmal` is not a smooth outer `feature`. It is the world-side carryover of
  the `Merkzeichen` structure.
- `Merkwelt` is not identical with `Umwelt`. It is only one side of the latter,
  and stays paired with `Wirkwelt`.
- `Umwelt` is not the whole circuit. In encounter 02 it is the coherent whole
  formed by `Merkwelt` and `Wirkwelt`, not yet the enclosing unity of inner and
  outer.
- `Funktionskreis` is not another synonym for `Umwelt`. It is the larger
  binding structure that wraps `Innenwelt` and `Umwelt` together.
- `Planmaessigkeit` is not one more item inside the chain. It names the mode of
  lawfulness or fitted reading under which the chain and circle are to be
  understood against merely causal description.

The secondary pressure surfaces keep this differentiation honest:

- `Wirkwelt` prevents the cluster from collapsing into a perception-only
  account.
- `Gegenstand / Objekt` prevents it from collapsing into neutral object-talk,
  because the worldly item is cut functionally, by leading properties, inside
  the circle.

## Does the cluster deserve explicit tracking as a reading unit?

Yes, at the bounded reading level.

This packet confirms the shift already visible in Experiment 03: `Umwelt`
still works as an entry node, but it no longer carries the decisive pressure in
isolation. The source now earns explicit tracking of the cluster as a **local
reading unit**, because the passages make the terms intelligible through their
ordered relation rather than as separate glossary items.

That judgment is narrower than an architecture decision. It means:

- the family should be read together when these passages are in play
- isolated replay on `Umwelt` alone is now likely to underdescribe the source
- the cluster is tracked because the source forces it, not because lexical
  grouping is attractive

It does not yet mean a new handle, a new glossary object, or a standing method
rule.

## New residue in current English tendencies

The packet makes a new residue visible: current English tendencies do not only
fail term by term. They also break the **serial construction** by which the
source binds the family.

- `feature` is too smooth for `Merkmal`, because it drops the carryover from
  `Merkzeichen`.
- `environment` or `surrounding world` make `Umwelt` look like a surrounding
  field instead of the result of `Merkwelt` and `Wirkwelt`.
- even `functional circle`, though still workable, can stand too independently
  in English and hide that the circle's world-side is internally articulated by
  the `Merk*` family.
- `planfulness`, if used loosely, can float above the chain as a general
  teleological mood instead of naming the lawfulness of the whole coupled
  structure.

So the new residue is not simply "no English term works." It is that English
pushes toward parallel nouns, while the source is building a stepped transfer
and enclosure. The smoother the English gets, the more easily that serial bond
disappears.

## Provisional Judgment

Packet 04 worked.

It beat the cheap result because it yielded:

- a stronger family bond than shared terminology
- a real differentiation statement instead of a generic cluster claim
- a bounded judgment that the cluster deserves tracking as a local reading unit

The smallest clean sentence the packet earns is:

> This family is Uexkuell's local construction for carrying one lawful fit from
> inner discrimination through worldly marking into organism-world circuitry.

That sentence is a result statement, not a settled doctrine.

## What this does not license

- not a settled English rendering for any member of the family
- not the collapse of the family into one generic doctrine of perception,
  subjectivism, semiosis, or teleology
- not promotion of the cluster into a new glossary object, handle, or canonical
  architecture unit
- not removal of `Umwelt` as a usable local node; it remains an entry point
- not a claim that `Planmaessigkeit` is now solved by any one English abstract
  noun
- not a full theory of Uexkuell's biology
- not widening this packet into Cassirer, biosemiotics, or a standing repo
  method
