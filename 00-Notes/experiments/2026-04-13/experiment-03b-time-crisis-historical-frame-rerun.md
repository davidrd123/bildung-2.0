# Experiment 03B: Time Crisis / Historical Frame Rerun

1. **Result statement**

   The contact still holds, but only at the level of temporal relation: Jünger and Dick both describe an inherited frame that no longer fits while a more-real order presses through it.

2. **Bridge sentence**

   Jünger carries the bridge in the breakdown of historical language at the `Zeitmauer`, where events no longer fit the old frame and the future begins to outline itself beyond it, while Dick carries the bridge in counterfeit time and the collapse of access from the subject side, where the world remains but the reader cannot receive it normally.

3. **Refusal sentence**

   Do not pair Jünger's `Zeitmauer` with Dick's orthogonal axis as if they were the same structure, and do not turn Dick's blindness or occlusion into a claim that the world itself is darkened.

4. **What carries the bridge on the Jünger side**

   The load-bearing detail is `§47`'s failure of the historical frame and inherited vocabulary, sharpened by `§48`'s dangerous naming of what appears beyond the wall.

5. **What carries the bridge on the Dick side**

   The load-bearing detail is Folder 21's counterfeit time and receptive pattern, with Folder 90's correction that the occlusion is from us rather than from the world.

6. **Provisional judgment**

   The packet works. It does not prove equivalence, but it does preserve a real bridge between frame-failure and a pressing order that exceeds the old regime of time.

7. **What the result does not license**

   It does not license a shared cosmology, a new handle, or a claim that threshold-language alone proves the same temporal structure on both sides.
