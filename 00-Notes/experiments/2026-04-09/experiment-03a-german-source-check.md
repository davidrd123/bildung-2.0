# Experiment 03a: German Source Check

Purpose: rerun the core claim of `experiment-03-passage-threshold-test.md` against the original German excerpts themselves rather than against the English draft phrasing.

Status: verification note. This is a stricter source check on Experiment 03, not a new free-standing experiment track.

## Question

Does the central result of Experiment 03 still hold when the comparison is made directly from the German passages?

More narrowly:

- is Cassirer's limit really methodological in the German itself
- is Junger's limit really both historical and methodological in the German itself
- does the negative around naming still hold once the German wording is foregrounded

## Packet

### Cassirer

`texts/erkenntnisproblem-vol1/parts/101-galilei-from-substance-to-function-and-phenomenal-knowledge.md`

German loci:

- `Bei Galilei erst wird der alte Satz: "operari sequitur esse" ... zu nichte. Er beginnt mit einer allgemeinen Gesetzlichkeit des Wirkens ...; erst unter ihrer Voraussetzung werden die Arten und Gattungen des Seins unterscheidbar.`
- `die Frage ist, ob mit den Dingen oder den Beziehungen, ob mit dem Dasein oder mit den Formen der Verknüpfung zu beginnen ist`
- `wir begnügen uns mit der Erkenntnis einiger ihrer empirischen Merkmale`

### Junger

`texts/zeitmauer/parts/16-humane-einteilungen.md`, `§48`

- `Dinge im Werden ... weil sie im Geschichtlichen nicht unterzubringen sind`
- `wo sich jenseits der Zeitmauer Zukünftiges abzeichnet`
- `In jeder Benennung schlummert Gefahr`

`texts/zeitmauer/parts/38-siderische-einteilungen.md`, `§114`

- `Die Frage wird diesseits und jenseits der Zeitmauer verschieden beantwortet`
- `Sie berührt den Standort oder die Standfläche unserer Untersuchung`
- the sciences `überschneiden sich` and `verzahnen sich`

## Method

1. Read only the German excerpts for the actual comparison.
2. Use the English drafts only afterward as a check on whether the existing phrasing had drifted.
3. Keep the same output discipline as Experiment 03:
   - one constrained sentence
   - one informative negative
   - what the reread strengthens or weakens

## Result

The central result of Experiment 03 survives the German-source check and is slightly strengthened.

The best constrained sentence is:

> Cassirer places the limit of valid order where inquiry still begins with `Dingen` and substantial essence instead of with `Beziehungen`, `Formen der Verknüpfung`, and a `Gesetzlichkeit des Wirkens`; Junger places it where what is coming to be is `im Geschichtlichen nicht unterzubringen` and where the same question must be answered differently `diesseits und jenseits der Zeitmauer` from another `Standort` or `Standfläche` of inquiry.

This is stronger than the English-only paraphrase for two reasons:

- the Cassirer side explicitly opposes `Dinge` to `Beziehungen` and `Dasein` to `Formen der Verknüpfung`
- the Junger side explicitly states both the historical insufficiency (`nicht unterzubringen`) and the methodological shift (`Standort oder Standfläche unserer Untersuchung`)

## What the German made clearer

### 1. Cassirer's limit is unmistakably methodological

The German is stronger than the paraphrase in three ways:

- `erst unter ihrer Voraussetzung werden die Arten und Gattungen des Seins unterscheidbar` makes the order of dependence explicit: lawfulness of operation comes first, the differentiations of being come second
- `ob mit den Dingen oder den Beziehungen ... zu beginnen ist` is not just a thematic contrast but a direct question about the starting point of inquiry
- `empirische Merkmale` confirms that the renunciation of inner essence is not skepticism but a discipline of admissible knowledge

So the core claim holds: Cassirer is not placing the limit beyond science, but inside the methodological conditions by which science can begin correctly.

### 2. Junger's limit is both historical and methodological in the German itself

`§48` and `§114` really do belong together.

`§48` gives the historical side:

- what is emerging cannot be housed within the historical
- the future draws itself beyond the wall
- naming this field too quickly is dangerous

`§114` gives the methodological side:

- the same question is answered differently on this side and beyond the wall
- the issue is explicitly tied to the footing of inquiry
- disciplinary partitions are shown as overlapping and interlocking rather than sovereign

So the Experiment 03 claim was not an English overreading. The German itself forces the conclusion that Junger's threshold changes both the field and the standpoint from which it is approached.

### 3. The strongest commonality remains anti-naive starting points, not shared doctrine

Both passages reject the sufficiency of inherited first appearances, but they do not do so in the same way.

- Cassirer rejects beginning from fixed things and substantial essence
- Junger rejects the sufficiency of the historical field and its inherited naming procedures

That is a real structural contact, but not a shared doctrine of language, science, or transcendence.

## Negative result

The German makes the earlier negative firmer:

Cassirer's movement away from `Worten` and toward a reformed concept does **not** pair cleanly with Junger's `In jeder Benennung schlummert Gefahr`.

Why the German confirms the mismatch:

- for Cassirer, the anti-scholastic turn is completed only when thought achieves a `neue Systematik des Begriffs`; the issue is not that naming is dangerous, but that concept must be methodically reformed
- for Junger, the issue is not mainly stale vocabulary; it is that what is coming to be is not yet historically containable, so naming can itself become a premature reduction

So the tempting bridge remains false even under the stricter source check.

## What this source check strengthens

- Experiment 03 was not leaning on a loose English phrasing for its main result
- the methodological character of both sides is stronger in the German than in the paraphrase
- the negative around naming is also stronger in the German than in the paraphrase

## What it still does not license

- not “Cassirer already contains the time wall”
- not “Junger is just doing a Germanized epistemology of science”
- not “both texts are making the same critique of language”
- not “the experiment track can now skip back to raw-German collision everywhere by default”

## Provisional Judgment

Experiment 03 passes the stricter test.

The result is now better grounded:

- Cassirer: methodological priority of relation, connection, and lawful operation over thing and essence
- Junger: historical and methodological insufficiency of the historical field on this side of the wall

That is enough to trust the bounded result, and also enough to stop here rather than widening the experiment track further.
