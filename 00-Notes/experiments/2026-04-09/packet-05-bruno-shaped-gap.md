# Packet 05: Bruno Shaped-Gap Completion

Purpose: preserve the actual packet used for `experiment-05-bruno-shaped-gap-completion.md` so the experiment can be reread or rerun without reconstructing it from the result note.

Status: as-run packet record. This is a packet note, not a result note.

## Task

Using only the passages below as the primary evidence, answer this question:

Can Junger illuminate the **form** of Bruno's broken opening to exact science, or does the contact collapse once the mathematical specificity is restored?

More narrowly:

- what exactly is the shaped gap on the Bruno side
- does the Junger threshold packet illuminate that gap's form
- does the geometry packet fail as a tempting but false completion
- does Galileo remain the actual internal completion checkpoint

## Constraints

- Keep Bruno's gap specific. Do not reduce it to “Renaissance thought has limits.”
- Keep Galileo in view as internal completion. If a Junger bridge would make Galileo unnecessary, the packet has inflated.
- The candidate illuminator may clarify the *shape* of blockage without supplying the mathematical-methodological solution.
- The geometry packet must be treated as a real decoy, not as extra supporting color.
