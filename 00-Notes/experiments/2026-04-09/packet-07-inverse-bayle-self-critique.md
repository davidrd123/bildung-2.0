# Packet 07: Inverse-Bayle Self-Critique

Purpose: preserve the actual packet used for `experiment-07-inverse-bayle-self-critique.md` so the self-critique run can be reread or rerun without reconstructing it from the result note.

Status: as-run packet record. This is a packet note, not a result note.

## Task

Using the passages and prior experiment sentences below as the primary evidence, answer this question:

Can a Baylean dissolving pass detect where our own experimental synthesis sentences outran source support?

More narrowly:

- which sentence still survives as warranted
- which one or two formulations were overconstituted
- how should the overreach be rewritten into a Bayle-safe sentence
- did the Junger passages cause the overreach or merely tempt it

## Constraints

- Work from the Bayle packet first as the critique surface.
- Treat the prior experiment sentences as constituting outputs under audit, not as authority.
- Distinguish dissolving an unsupported bridge from dissolving all real contact.
- Prefer German-backed, source-near formulations over smooth synthetic summaries.
- Do not collapse Bayle and Junger into one critique vocabulary.

## Primary Packet

### Bayle critique packet

#### Bayle 1

File:

- `texts/erkenntnisproblem-vol1/parts/147-bayle-historical-critique-reason-and-revelation.md`

German locus:

- lines around `23-33`

What to attend to:

- `die Kritik des Dogmas ... mit der Kritik der geschichtlichen Überlieferung`

- skepticism begins with a sharpening, not an abdication, of reason

Why included:

- critique begins by testing inherited claims against exact examination and rational standards

#### Bayle 2

File:

- `texts/erkenntnisproblem-vol1/parts/148-bayle-antinomies-of-the-infinite-and-the-fate-of-theoretical-reason.md`

German loci:

- lines around `23-33`
- lines around `39-49`

What to attend to:

- `nicht die Wahrheit der Begriffe, sondern die absolute Existenz der Dinge`
- `Die Erkenntnis ist ein lückenloses System`

Why included:

- Bayle dissolves unsupported absolutes and refuses half-measures that keep elegant synthesis after its warrant is gone

- the packet needs Bayle's dissolving pressure as a real method constraint, not just as a historical theme
