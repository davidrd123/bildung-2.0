## What This Check Confirms

  - Cassirer: chiefly methodological limit
  - Jünger: both historical and methodological limit
- The independent run produced a stronger negative than the local one. That is good evidence that the packet did not simply steer a canned answer.

## Method Lesson

The independent check matters as much as the result itself.

What made it useful:

- the packet was crafted separately from the result note
- the handoff excluded the earlier experiment prose
- the sub-agent was forced to stay at passage scale
- the comparison looked not only for convergence, but for a different informative negative

So the meaningful gain here is methodological:

- packet
- local run
- German source check
- independent handoff check

That is a much better control surface than the first experiment had, and it is the main thing worth improving if this track continues.
