# Packet 04: Reification Detection

Purpose: preserve the actual packet used for `experiment-04-reification-detection.md` so the experiment can be reread or rerun without reconstructing it from the result note.

Status: as-run packet record. This is a packet note, not a result note.

## Task

Using the passages below as the primary evidence, answer this question:

When does an ordering term remain regulative, opening, or diagnostic, and when does it harden into a self-standing content, power, or legitimating agent?

More narrowly:

- can Cassirer's reification pressure actually discriminate among Junger's ordering terms
- or does it just cast all late depth-language as suspicious

## Constraints

- Work from the selected passages first.
- Use control surfaces only to prevent flattening or term-equivalence.
- Do not generalize from one use of a term to all uses of that term.
- Require one hardened case, one productive/open case, and one clear non-case.
- Keep the lexical decoy in view: `Weltplan = Gesetz` should fail if the packet is honest.

## Primary Packet

### Cassirer discriminator packet

#### Cassirer 1

File:

- `texts/erkenntnisproblem-vol1/parts/115-descartes-extension-as-innate-idea-and-the-concept-of-substance.md`

German locus:

- lines around `23-33`

What to attend to:

- `nicht ... selbständige absolute Substanz des Körpers`
- `gleichsam ein gedankliches Substrat`
- the restriction to empirical natural appearances made measurable and intelligible
- `Verhältnissen und Funktionen` as the proper object of natural knowledge

Why included:

- positive baseline where the ordering condition remains methodological rather than becoming an existence-bearing support
