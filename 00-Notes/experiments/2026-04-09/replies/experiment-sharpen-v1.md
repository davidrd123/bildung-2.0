## 1) Reification-detection test

### Purpose

Test whether Cassirer’s reification pressure can actually discriminate between productive and hardened uses of ordering terms in Jünger, instead of merely redescribing all depth-language as suspicious.

### Baseline To Beat

Beat the cheap result that every use of `Weltplan`, `Urgrund`, or `Schöpfungsplan` is “already reified.”
This only counts if it produces at least:

* one clear productive / open case
* one clear hardened or at-risk case
* one real non-case

That matches the success/failure rule already stated in `live-options.md`.

### Question

When does an ordering term remain regulative or opening, and when does it harden into a self-standing content, power, or explanatory agent?

### Packet

#### Cassirer side: discriminating line

Use only these passages.

1. `texts/erkenntnisproblem-vol1/parts/101-galilei-from-substance-to-function-and-phenomenal-knowledge.md`
   Marker: paragraph beginning `Bei Galilei erst wird der alte Satz ...`
   Why included: positive non-reified baseline.

2. `texts/erkenntnisproblem-vol1/parts/120-descartes-collision-laws-galilei-and-the-transition-to-metaphysics.md`
   Markers:

   * paragraph beginning `Vom Standpunkt der Methodenlehre ...`
   * paragraph beginning `Wo dagegen die volle Identität ...`
     Why included: explicit test for the slide from method to metaphysics.

3. `texts/erkenntnisproblem-vol1/parts/141-malebranche-relation-divine-law-and-psychological-idealism.md`
   Marker: paragraph beginning `Ist somit für die empirische Wirklichkeit ...` through the divine-law turn
   Why included: law remains intelligible, then seeks efficacy in divine will.

4. `texts/erkenntnisproblem-vol1/parts/144-malebranche-eternal-truths-and-arnaulds-objection.md`
   Marker: paragraph beginning `In allen diesen Ausführungen ...`
   Why included: Cassirer states the reification formula explicitly.

#### Jünger side: target terms

1. `texts/zeitmauer/parts/37-siderische-einteilungen.md`, `§110`
   Why included: `Weltplan` / `Schöpfungsplan` in a strongly ordering register.

2. `texts/zeitmauer/parts/40-siderische-einteilungen.md`, `§118`
   Why included: explicit choice of `Urgrund` over `Weltgeist`.

3. `texts/zeitmauer/parts/49-siderische-einteilungen.md`, `§146-147`
   Why included: `Fortschritt im Weltplan`, research legitimation, and method-as-way.

4. `texts/zeitmauer/parts/57-urgrund-und-person.md`, `§175`
   Why included: built-in non-case candidate via the gap in `Weltplan`.

#### Support surfaces

* `texts/zeitmauer/source-bound-high-level-integration.md`
  Marker: section stating that `Urgrund` and `Weltplan` are strongest as reopened pressures, not settled doctrines.
* `texts/zeitmauer/threads/time-crisis.md`
  Markers: `zm:110:world-plan`, `zm:146:world-plan-research`, `zm:175:emptiness-not-nothing`

### German phrases that deserve attention

#### Cassirer

* `allgemeine Gesetzlichkeit des Wirkens`
* `ob mit den Dingen oder den Beziehungen`
* `Abstand zwischen Wahrheit und Wirklichkeit`
* `volle Identität zwischen Begriff und Sein`
* `Woher aber stammt die Wirksamkeit jener Gesetze selbst?`
* `die reine Funktion der Geltung`
* `ein besonderes, für sich bestehendes Objekt`
* `selbständige wirksame Kräfte`

#### Jünger

* `Der menschliche Plan wird umgrenzt ... von einem größeren: dem Schöpfungsplan`
* `in jedem Plan ein Regulativ verborgen`
* `das Wort "Urgrund" ... weil es ... zumutbarer ... als "Weltgeist" ist`
* `Fortschritt im Weltplan`
* `die Lücke, die kein Denken, kein Staatsplan füllt`

### Method

1. Distill a Cassirer test:

   * productive order stays methodological / regulative
   * reification begins when a condition of validity hardens into thing, force, or guarantor

2. Apply that test to each Jünger occurrence separately.

3. Force three labels:

   * open / regulative
   * at-risk / mixed
   * hardened

4. Require one explicit non-case.

### Expected decoy / negative

`Weltplan = reified Gesetz`
That should fail. The packet must test usage, not headword equivalence.

### What this does not license

* not “all Jünger depth-vocabulary is reified”
* not “Cassirer simply solves Jünger”
* not “Urgrund and Weltplan are one register”

### Difficulty

**Medium-high.**
It is runnable in one pass, but it is very easy to flatten Jünger into “suspect metaphysics.”

## 2) Shaped-gap completion test

### Purpose

Test whether a structurally marked gap in Cassirer can be illuminated by Jünger without pretending that Jünger supplies the missing solution.

### Baseline To Beat

Beat the vague result “both texts show limits.”
This only counts if it isolates:

* one specific shaped gap
* one plausible but false completion

### Question

Can Jünger illuminate the **form** of Bruno’s broken opening to exact science, or does the contact collapse once the mathematical specificity is restored?

### Packet

#### Cassirer side: gap and internal completion

1. `texts/erkenntnisproblem-vol1/parts/76-bruno-minimum-measure-and-the-irrational.md`
   Marker: opening diagnosis of `innere Antinomie`

2. `texts/erkenntnisproblem-vol1/parts/77-bruno-minimum-synthesis-and-boundary.md`
   Marker: section around `Minimum` / `Grenze`

3. `texts/erkenntnisproblem-vol1/parts/78-bruno-interval-pure-quantity-and-leibniz.md`
   Marker: opening correction beginning with `Wenn Minimum und Grenze ...`

4. `texts/erkenntnisproblem-vol1/parts/79-bruno-method-limit-and-exact-science-opening.md`
   Markers:

   * `Das "Mathematische" ist ... das "Mittlere"`
   * `mathematische Magie`
   * Phaedo / `logoi` / `pragmata`

5. `texts/erkenntnisproblem-vol1/parts/101-galilei-from-substance-to-function-and-phenomenal-knowledge.md`
   Marker: `Gesetzlichkeit des Wirkens` paragraph
   Why included: internal Cassirer-side completion checkpoint.

#### Jünger side: candidate illuminator

1. `texts/zeitmauer/parts/16-humane-einteilungen.md`, `§48`
2. `texts/zeitmauer/parts/17-humane-einteilungen.md`, `§51`
3. `texts/zeitmauer/parts/38-siderische-einteilungen.md`, `§114`

#### Jünger side: deliberate decoy subpacket

5. `texts/zeitmauer/parts/38-siderische-einteilungen.md`, `§112`

These are the “angle / row / cadastral / right-angle” packet.

#### Support surface

* `texts/erkenntnisproblem-vol1/reading/close-reading-ledger.md`
  Bruno entries for Parts `76-79`

### German phrases that deserve attention

#### Cassirer

* `innere Antinomie`
* `System qualitativ bestimmter und unterschiedener Einheiten`
* `Minimum und Grenze`
* `nicht in derselben Art als Quanta`
* `Intervall`
* `reine Kategorie der Quantität`
* `mathematische Magie`
* `verificare con la natura`

#### Jünger

* `im Geschichtlichen nicht unterzubringen`
* `jenseits der Zeitmauer`
* `In jeder Benennung schlummert Gefahr`
* `namen-los`
* `historische Benennung`
* `Standort oder die Standfläche`
* decoy phrases: `Winkelmaß und Reihung`, `Katasterblatt`, `rechter Winkel`

### Method

1. Reconstruct the Cassirer-side gap precisely:
   Bruno needs relation / interval / lawful boundary structure, but cannot house it inside discrete composition.

2. Mark the internal Cassirer completion:
   Galileo does not remove the gap atmospherically; he solves it methodologically.

3. Test whether Jünger’s threshold packet illuminates only the **shape** of a frame-overrun problem:
   something emerging cannot be lodged in inherited categories.

4. Then test the geometry decoy:
   if Jünger’s angle / row / cadastral world looks like Bruno’s completion, does the match survive return to Galileo?
   It should not.

### Expected decoy / negative

`Jünger’s angle / right-angle / cadastral world completes Bruno’s missing mathematics.`
That should fail.

### What this does not license

* not “Jünger solves Bruno”
* not “Jünger’s geometry is exact science”
* not “any vocabulary of limit counts as a completion”

### Difficulty

**High.**
This is the packet most likely to inflate into analogy unless the false completion is kept in view from the start.
