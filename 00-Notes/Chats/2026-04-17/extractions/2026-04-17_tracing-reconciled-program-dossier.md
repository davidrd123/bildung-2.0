## First orientation

The title is misleading.

The file starts as an etymology / cognacy / pre-PIE horizon chat, then becomes:

- a diagnosis of a missing semiotic-philological synthesis
- a large latent-space / Warburg-Cassirer-Peirce program script
- a repo-clone self-correction
- a second-order reflection on actual repo apparatus

The file's most useful parts are not the biggest program claims. They are the places where the chat:

- names a real epistemic limit
- proposes a bounded method
- gets forced back against actual repo material and corrects itself
