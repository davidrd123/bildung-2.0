## Strongest survivors from the folder

### 1. The anti-ornament filter is real

The most durable methodological gain in the large James/Nabokov chat is the `hackles` line:

- interpretation is not rejected
- interpretation that only grows more elegant inside its own home register is what gets refused

The stronger operational question becomes:

- can an interpretation survive contact outside its home register
- can it constrain, assist, or at least be answerable to neighboring domains

This is not a new repo principle from nowhere. It is a sharper expression of an already-live repo instinct. But it is strong enough to keep as working pressure.

### 2. `Integrator / gap-filler / re-evaluation` is a useful reading sort

The cleanest sorting move in the big program chat is the three-way distinction:

- **integrators**
  thinkers whose own work already holds multiple traditions in productive tension
- **gap-fillers**
  material that is not itself integrative in scope but answers a specific named shortfall in the current constellation
- **re-evaluation**
  material that originally looked like a gap-filler but may become more central once the constellation grows around it

This is better than vague "read broadly but with a filter" language because it ties reading decisions to:

- inheritance
- specific lack
- changed local fit

The related additions also seem worth keeping with it:

- the need to **name the gap first**
- the **assimilation problem**
  material may address a gap in content while still failing to assimilate into the apparatus because the register mismatch is too costly

This still belongs below promotion for now, but it is the strongest candidate from this folder for later reading-list discipline.

### 3. Reverse-engineering interpretive specs is a real method pressure

The Auerbach / Wind / interpretive-rigor section of the big chat contains a stronger method idea than I gave it credit for on the first pass:

- good interpretive work usually has specs
- older work often carries them implicitly rather than explicitly
- one can recover them by tracking:
  - what the author treats as evidence
  - what counter-readings are rejected
  - what scope limits are conceded
  - what kinds of claims are refused
  - how counter-example pressure is handled

This is a genuine methodological proposal, not just atmosphere. It is still untested in live repo work, but it is concrete enough to survive as pressure.

### 4. The "translation filter" turned into a wider projection / parallax apparatus

My earlier extraction understated the `Trust and safety` file. It is not just a side chat about job titles.

What survives there is the growth from:

- euphemism vs blunt rendering

to:

- multiple translation axes
  - lexical
  - structural / taxonomy-collapse
  - agent restoration
  - metaphor strip
  - Scarry/body-scale
  - causal chain
  - flow
  - historical residue
  - silence

and then into:

- the `N / n` or parallax-engine picture
  - a handle or phrase as compressed rendering
  - a lift toward a thicker referent-model
  - multiple re-projections along different axes
  - no single privileged rendering

This is not yet a repo method. It is still too hot, too general, and too untested. But it is more than side chatter. It is a live candidate perception apparatus.

### 5. Fischer is still the most concrete external candidate surfaced here

The folder's most specific outside reading candidate remains:

- **Eugen Fischer**, not Mark Fisher
- *Philosophical Delusion and its Therapy* (2010)

Why it survives:

- interpretive rigor held under empirical / cognitive pressure
- diagnostic and therapeutic handling of distorted pictures
- an actual attempt to integrate close reading and cognitive-scientific constraint

That makes it more concrete than most of the other name cascades in the folder.

## Pressure that seems real but should stay below promotion

### 1. Rival programs / a laboratory of research programs

The later James/Nabokov discussion goes well beyond cheap adversarial prompting and names something more serious:

- rival programs with their own ethos
- their own primary sources
- their own glossary and standards
- advocates built from inside those programs

This is not nonsense. It is a real future-method pressure.

But current honest status:

- promising
- structurally large
- unbuilt
- should not be operationalized from chat appetite alone

- controlled variation
- convergence/divergence maps
- pre-registered interpretive expectations
- evolutionary search over interpretive configurations

This is one of the most ambitious live proposals in the folder. It may eventually matter a great deal.

Current honest status:

- stronger than pure atmosphere
- still far above current earned practice
- should be treated as future methodological pressure, not present repo method

Some of this pressure is real. But most of it is still atmospheric production, not earned structure. The folder itself eventually begins to self-correct here, and that self-correction should be trusted.

### 4. McGilchrist, mode-pluralism, semiosis vs consciousness, and moral status

The large chat has real thought here, but it is unstable.
