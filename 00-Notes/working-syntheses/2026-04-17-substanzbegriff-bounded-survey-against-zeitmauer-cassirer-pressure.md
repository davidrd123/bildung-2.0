## Result

The tranche does add something real. Not a new Cassirer branch, but a sharper source-near pressure on three points.

### 1. Measure and experiment are constructive before they are flattening

The strongest addition comes from `0212`. `Erkenntnisproblem` already gave the historical arc toward function, law, and exact science. `Substanzbegriff` says more exactly what experimental and inductive science *does*:

- it does not accumulate raw observations
- it isolates conditions
- it constructs ideal cases
- it uses superposition and ordered relation-series to work back toward the complex actual case

That makes the first `Zeitmauer` constraint more exact. Measure is not merely positive in the weak sense of being useful before it becomes dangerous. It is constructive in the stronger sense that exact knowledge only exists through idealization, isolation, and recomposition.

This sharpens the `Zeitmauer` warning. Jünger's anti-measure pressure cannot be read as if science begins from naive registration and only later turns violent. Cassirer shows the constructive intervention is there from the start.

### 2. Objectivity is graded stability under criticism, not a fixed outside

`0221` adds a second sharpening. The subject/object split is not the starting point of knowledge, but a late outcome of differentiating experience by relative stability and criticism-resistance.

That matters for `Zeitmauer` because it makes the Cassirer pressure on `Weltplan` and related hardenings more exact:

- "objective" does not name a hidden outside standing over against thought
- it names what holds under stricter and wider conditions of coordination
- objectivity therefore comes in grades and can shift as the ordering work changes

This is stronger than the earlier note's general anti-reification warning. It gives a positive replacement: treat objectivity as a moving achievement inside experience, not as a thing-like domain waiting behind it.

### 3. Projection gives way to selection and apperceptive ordering

`0222` is the cleanest gain. Cassirer rejects the standard inside-to-outside projection story and replaces it with:

- selection inside experience
- privileged coordinate systems
- apperceptive ordering
- objective space as a logical result of ordered emphasis, not a projection from inner signs to outer things

This is the point most worth keeping. It gives a more precise background for several already-live repo pressures:

- the `Zeitmauer` problem of world-plan, cut, ordering, and preformatted seeing
- the Uexküll-side anti-psychologism / apperception pressure
- the broader predecided-world thread, if Cassirer is kept at the epistemic-historical level and not collapsed into organism-world or apparatus-world

The gain is not that Cassirer "explains" Jünger. The gain is that he gives a sharper account of how an ordered world can be constituted without resorting either to naive givenness or to metaphysical projection.

## Resistance / Non-Fit

The read was useful partly because it also stayed narrow.

- This tranche does **not** reach `Schicksalszeit`, qualitative time, or wall-crossing doctrine.
- It does **not** reach the apparatus / registry / uptake line in late `Zeitmauer`.
- It does **not** give a positive reading of `Urgrund`; it only keeps the anti-reification pressure live around any deep-ground move.
- It does **not** justify importing broader Cassirer vocabulary from outside the earned surface (`Symbolische Prägnanz`, `Energeia / Ergon`, `PSF` architecture, etc.).

So the contact is real, but it is still limited to constructive objectivity, methodological selection, and the anti-hardening pressure on explanatory terms.

## What This Changes

The bounded survey changes one thing in practice:

- the existing `Zeitmauer` Cassirer-pressure note can now be read more strongly on the measure / objectivity / selection line than before

It does **not** force a rewrite yet. The note on disk is still directionally right. This survey mainly shows that the pressure is better grounded than it looked from `Erkenntnisproblem` alone.

## Provisional Judgment

This was a legitimate bounded survey encounter.

It earned a real but local result:

- `Substanzbegriff` sharpens the existing Cassirer pressure on `Zeitmauer`
- especially around constructive method, graded objectivity, and selection over projection
- but it does not yet justify opening the source as a live campaign

The honest next move is therefore not "continue `Substanzbegriff`." It is to keep this result in the living layer and see whether later `Zeitmauer`, `Erkenntnisproblem Vol. II`, or Uexküll pressure actually calls for it again.
