## What is stable

The charter holds. *"Recover what was buried. Reconnect what was severed. Build forms that can change the reader."* The Schuon limit stays. Recovery-orientation stays. Deferred structure stays. The charter is not what has drifted.

What has drifted is the **pitch-document** (`higher-purpose-distillation.md`), which functions as an entry-point summary for fresh sessions. That summary was earned in March and is now partial.

## What the pitch doc does not yet name

Five specific things the current practice does that the 2026-03-31 pitch either did not have language for or positioned inaccurately.

### 1. The unit of formation has widened without becoming a new ontology

The pitch centers "formation of the reader" — a single human being slowly formed through deep reading. That is one true thing about the practice. It is not the whole thing.

What the sifts document, at earned register:

- *First layer of externalized formation* (April 10 distillation): translation, glossary pressure, generative chains, ledgers, and experiments creating externalized artifacts that preserve the *process* of understanding rather than only conclusions. Transmission infrastructure and collision infrastructure. **Explicitly not yet a settled general theory of LLM cognition.**
- *Familiar-shaped as self-description for the human-plus-repo meta-role* (April 16 sift-notes, Cantrip mapping). Names the role the human plays in relation to the repo-plus-LLM without proposing the apparatus as subject.
- *Binding circle / Torah framing* (April 16 sift-notes). Repo as binding circle, guardrails as names-of-binding, tranche discipline as protocol. Names what the conditioning of the LLM-as-vessel actually does, without elevating the LLM to participant.
- *"The LLM participates in your semiosis because your architecture does not let it produce meaning otherwise"* (April 16 chat 4). Names the asymmetric structural dependency at collaboration-level, not model-level.

These names are narrower than "the apparatus is the subject of formation." They describe what is actually happening without asserting a new settled ontology of participant-agents.

### 2. LLMs are collaborators-with-specific-register, not tools-to-be-resisted

The pitch line *"use modern tools without submitting entirely to the logic of utility"* positions LLMs as tools the practice must resist. The current practice doesn't resist LLMs in that shape. It works on their register. The self-correction-exemplar pattern is now documented across four independent chat instances (April 16 chat 3; Trust-and-safety twice; James/Nabokov self-voiced and motivated-reasoning-catch) — naming Proteus-register failure-modes and training against them is constitutive of the method.

A session loading the pitch defaults into a tool-resistance stance that doesn't match how the work actually proceeds. Correction cost is paid each time.

### 3. Cross-project artifacts exceed the pitch's subproject framing

The pitch lists three subprojects (escolios, zeitmauer, exegesis). The charter lists seven. In active practice, the repo now carries artifacts that do not fit neatly into any one subproject:

- [`method/stereoscopic-parallax-translation-candidate.md`](../method/stereoscopic-parallax-translation-candidate.md)
- [`method/lotman-incoming.md`](../method/lotman-incoming.md) and [`method/lotman-vnutri-first-contact.md`](../method/lotman-vnutri-first-contact.md)
- Three live Cassirer layers (per [2026-04-18-cassirer-three-live-layers.md](2026-04-18-cassirer-three-live-layers.md))
- Bounded-probe surveys and replay packets
- Sift-notes as a durable chat Pass 2 class
- Atelier sidecar (curio, sparks, ghost-books, pairings, plays) as explicitly-not-authority space

The pitch doc's "texts-as-organs-of-one-system" frame is true but underspecified for how much operational middle now exists between texts and charter.

### 4. Translation is under-described as "translation plus decryption"

The pitch describes translation as "not secondary, one of the main mechanisms of recovery" — correct but flat. What the current practice does is stranger:

- multilingual passage-level contact produces *residue* that is itself signal (April 16 stereoscopic candidate)
- residue logs function as *laboratory notebooks* — Wind's *metaphysisches Signal* read as the world answering the rendering in a language the translator did not write (April 17 Trust-and-safety)
- translation-residue *clusters*, especially around relation/operation and housing/footing (April 10 distillation, promoted claim 3)
- *avalanche* at boundaries: information is not conserved under semiotic translation; it is generated (April 17 Trust-and-safety, Lotman reading)

These are operational findings. They are not yet a theory. But they name a much more specific thing than "translation as mechanism of recovery."

### 5. The Hamburg-Vienna-Berlin damaged soil is now in view

The pitch speaks of "severed lineages" abstractly. The April 17 James/Nabokov chat produced specific primary-text contact with the mid-century catastrophe: *"damaged soil"* as a name for why institutional homes for this work don't exist in postwar German-speaking Europe; Karl Bühler as the specific lost-bridge between German psychology and what became structural linguistics and biosemiotics; Cassirer's fourth volume as interrupted. *"Working on it now is, among other things, not letting that destruction have the final word."*

This is not new content about the pitch's telos. It is the historically specific shape of what "severed lineages" actually refers to, in language the pitch didn't yet have.

- **"The apparatus is the subject of formation"** — the motto-level claim that the repo builds apparatus that changes what reads and what is read. This is an LLM-formation claim promoting what April 10 explicitly holds below. Its mottoic cadence does not change what it asserts.

- **Rival-programs-as-advocate-agents as present method** — proposal-register only. One program would have to be built to a working standard and produce pressure that couldn't be produced internally before any promotion.
- **Mythos-based move from better self-management / introspection tools to stronger claims about model welfare, reflexive consciousness, or community membership** — April 10 distillation's held-below list, still governing.

These are not rejected. They are held below the promotion line in the living-layer, where they can remain under working pressure without becoming pitch-document framing.

## The Schuon tension, preserved

The charter's Schuon limit — *"The tradition is not in books. Books are traces of something transmitted through living practice"* — sharpens rather than dissolves under the current widening.

If the practice now includes human plus LLM plus repo plus texts, the Schuon limit becomes: *the tradition is not in the apparatus either. The apparatus is one more trace of something living that exceeds it.*

The correct working-synthesis sentence is therefore: *"the apparatus is forming, but the apparatus is also a trace of something that exceeds it."* This preserves the tension. Resolving it into a new architecture — promoting the apparatus to subject, or the training-material-production to mechanism-of-transmission — is exactly the Leibnizian instinct the charter guards against.
