## Core distinction

The useful split in the chat was:

- **Type 1**: authors whose own work is already integrative and may be worth reading as inheritance material
- **Type 2**: authors or bodies of work that might address specific holes or partial cover in the current constellation, even if they are not "integrators" in the same sense

This note preserves that split as a personal-reading cohort only.

It does **not** say:

- these authors should be opened as repo campaigns
- these are the next items in `right-now.md`
- the gap claims made in the chat are already well-specified enough to license encounters
