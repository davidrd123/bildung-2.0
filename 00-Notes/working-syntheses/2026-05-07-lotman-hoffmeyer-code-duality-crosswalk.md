## Lotman Side

Lotman's mechanism in §3 begins from heterogeneous consciousness. A minimal
thinking device must contain at least two differently organized systems that
exchange texts. One system is discrete: it builds linear chains of segments,
with the sign as primary unit. The other is continual: the whole text carries
meaning, and meaning is spread across a multidimensional semantic space.

Exact translation between these systems is impossible. A discrete semantic unit
corresponds on the continuous side to a blurred semantic field rather than a
matching segment. But the attempt at translation is precisely where new
semantic relations arise. The trope names a contextual adequacy relation
between mutually noncomparable meaningful elements.

The mechanism is cultural-cognitive: heterogeneous codes exchange texts, and
creative thought emerges from approximate, context-bound translation across
their incompatibility.

## Hoffmeyer Side

Hoffmeyer distinguishes digital codes from analog codes. Digital codes use
discrete sign tokens with arbitrary, conventional, or historical relations to
content. Analog codes work through similarity, spatio-temporal continuity,
part-whole relation, or cause-effect relation. He treats the genetic code as a
clear digital code and the living organism's embodied interaction in ecological
space as analog codification.

Code-duality is the recursive exchange between those two modes. Digital coding
gives living systems temporal stability, memory, combinatorial freedom, and
meta-message capacity. Analog coding gives world interaction, other-reference,
preference, and actual realization in space and time. The digital description
must be translated into an analog organism through development, and this
requires active cellular organization or tacit knowledge; the digital code alone
is not a total description of the organism.

The mechanism is organismic and evolutionary: life persists through the
interdependence of digital memory and analog world-coupled action.

## Shared Operator

The overlap is real:

- both reject a one-code or pure-transmission model;
- both require at least two differently organized semiotic modes;
- both align one side with discrete/symbolic segmentation and the other with a
  continuous or iconic-indexical field;
- both make translation between modes productive rather than merely
  reproductive;
- both resist treating code as a context-free lookup table;
- both see new information or new patterns arising from the relation between
  non-identical systems.

At this level, the shared operator is:

```text
heterogeneous code systems + non-identical translation = generative meaning or
adaptive novelty
```

That is a strong analogy.

## Why It Is Not Identity

The mechanisms do not have the same domain or the same load-bearing terms.

Lotman §3 works at the level of cultural and cognitive semiosis. His discrete /
continual pair can include word and image, formula and model, scene and verbal
description, scientific analogy and poetic trope. The result is new meaning,
new semantic relation, or a new text.

Hoffmeyer works at the level of life cycles, heredity, development, and
evolution. His digital / analog pair is tied to DNA or RNA memory, cellular
interpretive machinery, organismic embodiment, ecological interaction, and
lineage-level survival. The result is semiotic survival, developmental
realization, and evolutionary novelty.

Lotman's continuous side is a semantic field whose segmentation resists exact
mapping. Hoffmeyer's analog side is not simply continuous; it is iconic and
indexical, materially coupled to world processes, and active in the organism's
here-and-now interaction.

Lotman does not, in §3, require heredity, a protected digital memory substrate,
genotype / phenotype translation, or organism-environment coupling. Hoffmeyer
does. So the crosswalk should not say that Lotman already has Hoffmeyer's
code-duality. It should say that Lotman's heterogeneous translation mechanism
has a strong analogue in Hoffmeyer's biosemiotic code-duality.

## Practical Use

Use Hoffmeyer to discipline one temptation in Lotman: do not let "continuous"
become a vague aesthetic word. Hoffmeyer's analog side is world-coupled,
indexical, iconic, and active.

Use Lotman to discipline one temptation in Hoffmeyer: do not let code-duality
collapse into a biology-only genotype/phenotype scheme. Lotman shows the same
operator at work in rhetoric, art, scientific modeling, and cultural meaning.

The relation is therefore a constrained crosswalk:

- mechanism layer: strong analogy
- ontology: different
- domain: different
- promotion status: hold
