# PSF I Chapter V — What It Changes in the Cassirer Map

Status: short source-near update. Not a whole-author synthesis. Not a
promotion of `Philosophie der symbolischen Formen` above the presently
source-earned `Substanzbegriff` line.

## Why this note exists

The repo already had a three-layer Cassirer map:

1. `Erkenntnisproblem` / operational historical Cassirer
2. `Substanzbegriff` / systematic constructive Cassirer
3. `PSF I` / language as constitutive objectivation

What chapter V changes is not the existence of a third layer.

It changes the **job description** of that third layer.

## The change

Before chapter V, the active reason to keep `PSF I` alive was mostly:

- language as constitutive objectivation
- world-view difference is internal to language
- symbolic form is not a loose perspective term

All of that remains true. But it still left `PSF I` sounding adjacent to
`Substanzbegriff`: relation, constitution, objectivity, only now with more
language-pressure.

Chapter V sharpens the difference.

`Substanzbegriff` is strongest where the question is:

- how objective order is constructed
- how relation replaces substance in scientific object-constitution
- how objectivity is won without naive givenness

`PSF I` chapter V becomes strongest where the question is:

- how a symbolic medium bears relation at all
- how judgment is articulated linguistically
- how pure predicative synthesis is historically and structurally won
- how relation moves from material embedding toward explicit expression

So the third Cassirer layer is no longer just:

- language as world-forming objectivation

It is now more exactly:

- **language as the medium-specific history of relation-expression**

That is what chapter V adds to the map.

## What this changes in practice

The decision rule becomes cleaner.

Use `Substanzbegriff` when the live question is:

- constructive objectivity
- scientific method
- graded stability, selection, apperception, objectivation

Use `PSF I` when the live question is:

- whether relation is being carried by the medium itself
- whether sentence, syntax, hypotaxis, copula, or predication are the real
  site of the problem
- whether a pressure is not just about constituted objects, but about the
  articulation conditions under which relation can appear

This does not make `PSF I` the new center.

It makes it a **more exact reserve surface**.

## What did not change

- `Substanzbegriff` remains the current source-earned Cassirer center.
- `PSF I` is still not a campaign.
- Volume II and Volume III remain below activation.
- `symbolic form` is still not licensed as a repo-wide master term.
- There is still no direct payoff here for `Schicksalszeit`.
- This still does not solve the Jünger naming-crisis material by itself.

## Short judgment

Chapter V did not widen Cassirer.

It **differentiated** him.

After this tranche, the repo no longer has to say only:

- `PSF I` is the language layer

It can say something more operational:

- `Substanzbegriff` = constructive objectivity
- `PSF I` chapter V = medium-specific relation-expression

That is a real improvement in the Cassirer map, and it is earned from the page.
