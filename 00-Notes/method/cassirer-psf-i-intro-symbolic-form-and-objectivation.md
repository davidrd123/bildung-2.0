## What the introduction actually does (at this grain)

This is the “why symbolic forms?” setup, but it starts by tightening an already-repo-familiar move:

- Scientific concepts are not passive copies; they are **self-created symbols** whose value lies in the unity they produce (Hertz/Helmholtz line in `0101`).
- Objectivity is not “unmediated access”; every objectivation is mediation (`0101`, “Objektivierung … Vermittlung”).
- Different “media” / conceptual structures yield different object-senses even within “nature” (physics vs chemistry vs biology) (`0101`).
- The demanded “unity of knowledge” is no longer unity of substrate; it becomes a **functional unity** of distinct methods (`0101`).

Then the step that matters for the repo’s “don’t overfit science” discipline:

- Knowledge is only one form of spiritual formation. Art, myth, religion are also “Objektivierungen,” but they reach general validity by different routes than the logical concept (`0101`).
- Each basic spiritual function has an original *forming* power (not merely reproducing) and constitutes a “side of the real” (`0101`).

In `0102`, Cassirer explicitly routes this through a Kant line:

- Kant’s “revolution of thought” generalizes: not just primacy of judgment-function over object, but a broader **primacy of function over product** across cultural domains (`0102`, “Primat der Funktion”).
- “Die Kritik der Vernunft wird damit zur Kritik der Kultur.” (`0102`)
- Culture implies “Sein” is graspable only in “Tun” (`0102`).

He ends with a real methodological dilemma and a required “middle term”:

- If we force unity by logic alone, we crush the autonomy of the domains; if we stay with domain-individuality alone, we lose systematic connection.
- The only exit is to find a “moment” that appears in every basic form but not in the same shape, and a “medium” through which all formation passes while preserving specificity (`0102` end).

At this grain, the introduction is not yet “here is the entire symbolic-forms system.” It is:

- a constraint against naive copy-theory
- a constraint against single-domain monopoly (logic/science as prototype)
- a demand for a mediating notion that can carry both systematic unity and domain autonomy

## What this sharpens (repo use)

When the repo reaches for “symbolic forms,” this intro gives a concrete calibration:

- “symbolic form” is not a loose synonym for “framework” or “perspective”
- it is trying to name **medium-specific objectivation**: how different cultural energies form objectivity/meaning in different ways

So this intro is the right surface when the live question is:

- are we dealing with “objectivity” only in the scientific-method register, or is the medium itself doing constitutive work?
- are we smuggling “a single true object” back in by treating one register as the prototype?
