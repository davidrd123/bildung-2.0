## Inherits

Use the general spine-author dossier discipline first. This note only adds
Lotman-specific sorting, risks, and first targets.

## Governing Judgment

Lotman is strongest as a case-driven theorist of cultural semiosis. Use him as
a source of mechanisms and historical-literary hypotheses, not as a master
metalanguage for the repo.

Stay close to his worked examples. When a concept cannot be tied to a concrete
Lotman example, leave it tentative.

## Strongest Source Zones

- Concrete literary-historical readings: Pushkin, Decembrist behavior, Russian
  cultural history, Gogol, Karamzin, theater, law, honor, mercy.
- Chapter-scale work in *Kultura i vzryv*: duel, Chaplin, art, dream, death,
  proper names, binary/ternary transition.
- Source-facing mechanisms in *Vnutri myslyashchikh mirov*: minimal paired
  heterogeneity, autocommunication, semiosphere/boundary/dialogue, history as
  decryption.
- Text as generative device: track inputs, transformations, outputs, memory,
  and later re-readings without reducing the text to author psychology.

## Softer Zones

- Semiosphere as total theoretical entity.
- Binary/ternary as cultural typology.
- High-abstract autocommunication when detached from source cases.
- Tartu institutional self-narrative and meta-discourse.

These may orient reading. They do not pass as mechanisms without source-bound
examples and scope limits.

## Candidate Mechanisms

These are the Lotman concepts most eligible for dossier work:

- translation / untranslatability
- boundary as translation membrane
- text within text
- self-description
- explosion as bounded unpredictability
- retrospective transformation of chance into necessity
- dynamic asymmetry of memory
- code plus history
- proper name / common noun switching
- art as experiment in freedom
- autocommunication as self-restructuring under a second code
- history as decryption of texts produced under other codes

## Diagnostic Lenses

Keep these useful but non-foundational:

- binary / ternary cultural structure
- Russia / West
- law / mercy
- sex and death as cultural languages
- utopia / apocalypse
- gradualism / explosion as civilizational temperament

These lenses can guide noticing. They cannot carry a claim unless a dossier
shows the actual operation at work in bounded examples.

## Lotman Dossier Pass Criteria

A Lotman concept earns ongoing use only if it has:

1. Two distinct Lotman worked examples, preferably from different domains.
2. A clear mechanism, or an explicit demotion to diagnostic lens.
3. A stated scope limit.
4. A comparator that constrains the same analytical layer.
5. A named overreach risk.
6. A source-status note saying whether the passage is reader-en only,
   cleaned-Russian backed, or page-image checked.

Until then it remains tentative.

## Comparators By Layer

### Mechanism

- Peirce: sign relation, interpretation, habit, tychism.
- Eco: model reader, encyclopedia, cooperation, limits of interpretation.
- Hoffmeyer or biosemiotics: organism-level precedent for boundary,
  translation, code, and historical accumulation.
- Whitehead: asymmetry of past and future, process, concrescence, novelty.

### Historical Diagnosis

- Bakhtin: culturally grounded dialogism, chronotope, carnival, polyphony.
- Karamzin and Klyuchevsky where Lotman is making Russian-historical claims.
- Comparative non-Russian cases when Lotman risks converting Russian patterns
  into universal cultural laws.

### Literary Reading

- Auerbach: standard for large claims earned through close reading.
- Tynyanov and Jakobson: formalist constraints near Lotman's own inheritance.
- Bakhtin where the shared text-field includes novel, Dostoevsky, Rabelais, or
  speech plurality.

## First Model Dossier

Start with `retrospective inevitability / dynamic asymmetry of memory`.

Why:

- Worked examples are abundant: duel before and after the shot, historian as
  prophet predicting backward, theater spectator seeing a play for the second
  time, revolutions narrated afterward as inevitable, artistic endings forcing
  rereading of beginnings.
- Mechanism is clear: forward time presents a fan of possible futures; backward
  memory compresses the realized path into necessity.
- Scope can be stated: strongest wherever there is temporal observer position,
  weaker for purely synchronic analysis.
- Comparators are real: Peirce on chance, Whitehead on past/future asymmetry,
  Eco on interpretive constraint, historical method on contemporaneous versus
  retrospective evidence.
- Overreach risk is visible: treating all historical narration as falsification
  rather than distinguishing careful reconstruction from retrospective myth.
