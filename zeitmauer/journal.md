# Translation Journal

## 2026-03-29 - Scaffold

Set up a dedicated `zeitmauer` project rather than forcing this book into the `escolios` shape.

Reasons:

- the source text is already clean enough to extract directly from the ebook text
- the main work is divided into `186` numbered prose units
- the appended adnotes should be tracked separately, not mixed into the main translation corpus

Initial structure:

- reproducible extractor for `sections.yaml`
- working glossary for title decisions and recurring conceptual terms
- translation batches under `parts/`

## 2026-03-29 - Calibration Batch

Started with `§1-3` under `parts/01-fremde-voegel.md`.

Open decisions at this stage:

- whether `An der Zeitmauer` should remain untranslated at the project level
- whether `Schicksalszeit` should be rendered as `fateful time`, `destinal time`, or something else
- whether `mantisch` should remain `mantic` or be smoothed to `divinatory`
- how aggressively to keep Jünger's long syntax intact in English

Current bias:

- keep the English dignified but readable
- preserve Jünger's analogical movement and conceptual repetition
- avoid flattening difficult terms too early
