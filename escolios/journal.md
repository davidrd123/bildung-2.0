# Translation Journal

## 2026-03-29 — Starting

Source: OCR text + PDF scan of *Escolios a un texto implícito I* (1977).

Starting with the first 15 aphorisms (pp. 11-13). These are the opening moves — Gómez Dávila setting the terms of engagement. Several are meta-textual: about the form of the book itself, about how to read it, about the relationship between brevity and truth.

Working method: two translations per aphorism (close/free), then notes and exegesis. Existing translations at don-colacho.blogspot.com as reference — to compare after drafting, not before.
